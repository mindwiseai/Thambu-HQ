# GT Pro Rig — RaceSims Flagship Product

The GT Pro Rig is [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]]' primary assembled product — an aluminium-profile sim racing cockpit that serves as the foundation for all kit configurations. It appears across quotes, kit pricelists, and the [[araya-airport]] B2B builds.

## Variants (from quotes and pricelists)

| Configuration | Description | Approx. MRP (INR) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------------|
| GT Rig (single screen) | Base cockpit + single monitor | Starting kit ~2.5L |
| GT Rig (triple screen) | Base cockpit + triple 32" monitors | Mid-range kit ~4-5L |
| Formula Cockpit | Open-wheel seating position + formula wheel | ~5-6L |
| Formula Cockpit with Motion | Formula + 3DOF motion platform | ~11L+ |

## RaceSims own components

RaceSims manufactures/sources its own:
- **GT rig frame** (aluminium profile cockpit) — Rs.38,500-42,350
- **Racing seats** — Fixed back, Fixed back XL, Recliner, Recliner Sports (Rs.45,500 in Araya builds)
- **Monitor stands** (single + triple) — Rs.30,000 in Araya builds
- **Exterior cladding** (for commercial builds like [[araya-airport]]) — ~Rs.90,000

## Next generation

[[mr-leela-krishnan]] (boss at [[arka-motorsports]]) is mentoring [[thambu]] on **cast aluminum bracket and mounting hardware design** for [[next-gen-racesims-rigs]] — a significant product evolution beyond the current aluminium-profile approach.
