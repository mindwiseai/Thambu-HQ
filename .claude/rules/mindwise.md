# Mindwise — Project Rules

These rules apply when you are working on anything Mindwise-related: ingesting meeting notes, drafting marketing copy, editing CTD documents, answering questions about the launch.

## Brand essentials (always-on context)

- **Product:** Single SKU — CDRI-08 standardised Bacopa monnieri extract, capsule form (60 caps / 100 ml bottle for D2C, 30-cap blister for B2B)
- **Tagline:** "Your Unfair Advantage" (LOCKED — see [[your-unfair-advantage]])
- **Campaign hook:** "When the Brain is Sharp, Learning Becomes a Child's Play"
- **Brand enemy:** "Placebo Wellness" — pretty labels, weak dosages, borrowed research
- **Visual:** semi-transparent bottle showing green capsules + monochrome (charcoal-gray / blue) + green pop. Logo Route 3 ("Architecture of Intelligence")
- **Launch:** June 1, 2026 (full market launch June–July)
- **Pricing:** ₹850–900 MRP locked direction (see [[pricing-strategy]])
- **Owner:** Lumen Marketing Company. Founder: Thambu. Brand Manager TBD (see [[brand-manager-decision]])
- **Agency:** [[sorted-agency]] (Aarti, Manik, Tanya, Binita, Avantika, Ankita)

## Hard rules — DO NOT VIOLATE

1. **NO marketing claim without CTD backing.** Every clinical claim ("improves memory", "55% bacosides", "7 trials") must trace to a source page in `wiki/sources/` that points to the CTD Module 2 documents. The FSSAI compliance risk is real and the brand has been positioned as government-backed — overclaiming destroys both.

2. **CDRI-08 is NOT generic Bacopa.** Always distinguish. Generic Bacopa is 20–30% bacosides; CDRI-08 is 55%+. Conflating them kills the differentiation.

3. **Voice/tone:** confident but approachable, educational but not preachy, performance-driven but human, scientific but accessible. NEVER scary, medicinal, or prescription-like. (See [[positioning-shift]] for why this matters — the entire pivot was away from clinical/scary.)

4. **Communication style with Sorted Agency:** detailed, structured, vendor-aware. Thambu's working pattern with Sorted is to send numbered feedback (see `mindwise/Meeting Notes/message-to-binita-packaging-feedback.md` for the canonical example). When drafting comms to Binita / Manik / Aarti, follow that shape.

5. **Pricing discipline:** never propose discounting on single-bottle MRP. Bundle discounts only. Heavy discounting destroys premium positioning.

- Don't propose new strategic directions unless asked. The strategy is locked; execution is the job.
- Don't conflate Sorted Agency people (see `wiki/entities/` for who does what).
