# CSIR-CDRI (Central Drug Research Institute)

Government of India research institute (under CSIR — Council of Scientific & Industrial Research). Located in Lucknow. **The original developer and patent-holder of the standardized [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 Bacopa monnieri extract]]** that powers [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]].

## Why it matters

- **Government backing.** This is the entire basis for Mindwise's positioning as "the only government-backed CDRI-08 brain supplement." Generic Bacopa brands cannot make this claim.
- **40+ years of research.** CDRI's work on the extract goes back to the 1960s — pre-clinical pharmacology, toxicology, human safety, clinical trials.
- **Patented extraction.** CDRI developed and standardized the unique extraction process that yields 55%+ bacosides (vs. 20-30% in generic Bacopa).
- **Continuing relationship.** [[dr-hemant-singh]] and others from CDRI remain scientific advisors / mentors.

## Regulatory framing for Mindwise

- "Originated at CSIR-CDRI, Lucknow"
- "Government-backed brain supplement"
- TGA-approved in Australia (where the extract first commercialized as KeenMind / Mimad — see [[adam-ritson]])
