### Key Origin Story Discussed
**The Adam Ritson Catalyst (Late 1990s-Early 2000s):**
- News clipping in Times of India (early 1990s): CDRI working on Bacopa monnieri extract
- Thambu's company approached CDRI about commercializing the research
- CDRI researchers (Dr. Dhawan, Dr. Hemant Kumar Singh) were gatekeepers—required more research validation
- Year 2000: CDRI completed ADHD clinical trials, gave commercialization go-ahead
- Olga Sortal from Sydney learned about the extract; her nephew Adam Ritson suffered severe brain damage from a sports accident
- Olga gave Adam high doses experimentally (not doctor-approved)—he showed remarkable recovery
- Media sensation in Australia (late 1990s/early 2000s) → Olga commercialized as "Mimad" brand
- Peak sales: 100,000 packs/month in Australia
- Product then went global: KeenMind (Australia/South Africa/Middle East), MemoPlus Gold (Philippines), Memory Perfect (Europe), Actif Mind (Thailand/Malaysia)

### Brand Purpose Articulated
**Central Brand Idea (Workable Platform Line):**  
> "Think for your brain today, so your brain can think for you tomorrow."

**Brand Positioning Emerging:**
- Not a quick-fix booster; long-term habit for cognitive wellness
- Core feeling: "I can do more with this product" (mental performance angle, not hype)
- Target: High performers wanting to level up, students, professionals, aging populations
- Key demographic insight: Age 30-45 as primary consumer/customer; 60+ secondary (elderly)
- Age bracket starting at 18-20 with high performer emphasis

### USP Clarification Crystallized
1. **Original CDRI-08 extract** — not generic bacopa (most competitors use 20-30% bacosides; Mindwise = 55%+)
2. **7+ gold-standard clinical trials** — improvements in memory, learning, information processing, attention, anxiety/stress
3. **Government backing** — CSIR-CDRI originated (unique regulatory advantage)
4. **Patented extraction process** — seed-to-consumer quality control with batch consistency
5. **Long safety margin** — 40 years of pre-clinical toxicology + real-world use overseas
6. **Global trust record** — Millions using globally for 20+ years

### Four Target Audiences Defined
1. **Students & Exam-Goers (18-24):** UPSC, JEE, board exams; personally/parent-motivated
2. **High-Pressure Professionals (25-45):** Cognitive load, sustained performance, non-stimulant alternative
3. **Proactive Agers & Caregivers (40+):** Memory decline, dementia prevention
4. **ADHD Individuals (18+):** Focus and cognitive stability
