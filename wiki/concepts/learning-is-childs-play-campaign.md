# "Learning is Child's Play" — Kids Cohort Campaign

Campaign 1 of the [[four-cohorts|four-cohort]] content rollout. Designed in [[2026-02-12-meeting-5-content-strategy|Meeting 5]] (Feb 12, 2026). Lead facilitator: [[tanya-leeds]].

## Central campaign hook (cross-cohort umbrella)
> **"When the Brain is Sharp, Learning Becomes a Child's Play"**

This hook works across all four cohorts but is most literal here.

## Insight
- Kids are naturally curious and ask lots of questions
- **Working memory and attention are stronger predictors of academic success than IQ**
- [[cdri-08|Mindwise]] improves focus, attention span, memory → learning becomes easier
- Not about being "born smarter" — it's about having better attention/memory

## Brand Film Script (locked direction)

Series of kids in everyday scenes showing evidence of improved learning:

1. Kid coloring shark, tells dad: *"Dad, you know sharks were alive then..."*
2. Girl brushing hair: *"Didi, you know our hair grows faster when we sleep"*
3. Boy looking at sky: *"Dadu, moon pena moon wake moon quakes"*
4. Two kids walking: exchanging quick facts with retention
5. Kid with pencil upside down: *"Mom, you know pencils can't write underwater"* (then drops in bucket)
6. Small girl with dog: *"Did you know dogs can smell our feelings"*
7. Kid with mom's phone, whispers seriously: *"Your brain uses more energy than your phone"*

## VO

> "When the brain is sharp, learning becomes a child's play.
> Mindwise supports brain health, focus, memory, and cognitive performance.
> Now learning is a child's play."

## Key Visuals (static ad direction)
- Images that grab attention (kids in cute scenes)
- Headline: campaign hook
- Subheadline: "Only government-backed brain supplement for memory, concentration, focus"
- Product image + CTA ("Buy Now")

## Sustenance content
- UGC from mom creators showing kids thriving
- Tips for helping kids focus and learn better
- Testimonials from parents noticing improvements
- Brain development facts (working memory > IQ etc.)

## Marketing target
- Audience: parents, especially **mom creators** for influencer activation
- Buyer: parent (mostly mom)
- Beneficiary: child
- This is not a kid-targeted ad; it's a *parent-targeted ad about kids*

## Caveats / regulatory notes

⚠️ Mindwise CANNOT make medical or treatment claims for ADHD, autism, or learning disorders even though there's historical international use ([[adam-ritson|Philippines / MemoPlus Gold]] case). Stick to **focus, attention, learning support** language. Frame as a daily wellness habit, not a treatment.

⚠️ Per [[2026-03-23-binita-packaging-feedback]] feedback point 8: dosing for children 7+ is **1 capsule per day** (vs. 2/day for adults). Any kids-cohort content that mentions dosing must reflect this.
