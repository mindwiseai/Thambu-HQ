 REEL Braking Technique: Trail Braking Master
Click to expand caption & hashtags →
📝 Caption:
Trail braking separates amateurs from aliens. Here's why: most drivers brake in a straight line, then turn. Champions brake while turning. You carry more speed into the corner because your braking zone extends further. Your front tires have grip left—use it. The technique: hard initial braking, progressive release into the corner apex, smooth throttle application on exit. It's a dance. Your brake pressure decreases as your steering angle increases. This is THE move at Monaco, Suzuka, and every high-speed circuit. Watch how this translates in real-time telemetry. Your pedal curve matters here. Good resolution, responsive feel, and confidence in your equipment is essential. This is why we stock Conspit pedals—they're built for this level of precision. Want to learn more? Our engineers are here to help you nail this technique.
