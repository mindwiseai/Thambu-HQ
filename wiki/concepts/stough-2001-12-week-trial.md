# Stough et al. 2001 — Original 12-Week Australian Trial

The **first major Western clinical trial** of standardized [[cdri-08|CDRI-08]] extract. Foundational citation for the entire Mindwise evidence base.

## Citation

> Stough C, Lloyd J, Clarke J, Downey LA, Hutchison CW, Rodgers T, Nathan PJ. (2001). The chronic effects of an extract of *Bacopa monniera* (Brahmi) on cognitive function in healthy human subjects. *Psychopharmacology*, 156(4):481-484.

## Study design

- **Format:** Double-blind, placebo-controlled, randomized
- **Sponsor:** Mindwise Pty Ltd
- **Product:** "Mindwise" — Standardized ethanolic Bacopa monnieri extract (CDRI Lucknow) — 55%+ combined bacosides ([[55-percent-bacosides]])
- **Dose:** 2 × 150 mg capsules / day (= 300 mg/day)
- **Site:** Neuropharmacology Laboratory, Brain Sciences Institute, Swinburne University, Hawthorn, Victoria, Australia
- **Duration:** 12 weeks
- **Participants:** 46 healthy volunteers (n=23 active, n=23 placebo)
- **Inclusion:** male/female 18-60, healthy, 48-94 kg, informed consent
- **Exclusion:** pre-existing physical/psychiatric condition, any medications

## Methodology

Wide neuropsychological battery:
- Cognometer working memory tests
- Digit Symbol Substitution
- Speed of Comprehension Test
- Digit Span
- Trail Making Test
- Rey Auditory Verbal Learning Test
- Inspection Time
- State anxiety scale

Plus weekly symptom tracking (positive + negative).

## Findings (the citable claims)

### Significant improvements after 12 weeks
- ✅ **Speed of visual information processing** — improved
- ✅ **Learning rate** — improved
- ✅ **Memory consolidation** — improved
- ✅ **Decreased proactive interference**
- ✅ **Decreased forgetting rate**
- ✅ **Decrease in state anxiety** (anxiolytic effect)

## Authors' commentary
- Cognitive effects may be mediated by:
  - Modulation of cholinergic system
  - Potent antioxidant effects
- Anxiolytic effect possibly mediated via serotonergic system
- Recommended further studies with wider batteries, different doses, age-related effects

## Significance

This is the trial that **established the 12-week timeline** that Mindwise inherits as its [[8-12-week-onset-claim|8-12 week onset window]]. It also establishes:
- The 300 mg/day dose (= 2 capsules of 150 mg)
- The brand name "Mindwise" formally tied to CDRI-08
- The standardized 55% bacoside extract as the test product

## Quality
- Smaller cohort (46) than [[stough-2008-90-day-trial|Stough 2008]]
- More positive findings (broader effect spread)
- Stronger anxiety effect than the 2008 follow-up
- Sister trial: Stough's 2008 study refined methodology with longer duration and CDR computerized assessment
