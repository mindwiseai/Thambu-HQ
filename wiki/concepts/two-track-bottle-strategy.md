# Two-Track Bottle Strategy

A risk-mitigation approach for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] packaging, locked in [[2026-03-17-meeting-binita-strategic-decisions|the March 17 meeting]] with [[binita]]. Protects the [[launch-plan-june-1-2026|June 1 launch]] regardless of vendor outcomes.

## The two tracks

### Track 1 — Aspirational Design (exploration)
- Premium flush cap (cap meets bottle seamlessly, no visible neck)
- Multiple shapes to explore: cylindrical, square (AceBlend Omega-3 ref), rectangular
- **Challenge:** square/rectangular bottles nearly impossible to source in India without expensive custom molds + high upfront costs
- **Sources:**
  - China sourcing: 1-week delivery if exact specs provided (proven via AceBlend = sourced from Alibaba/China)
  - [[patco-pharmaceuticals|Patco]] (India): customization vendor identified mid-March that can do BOTH bottle + cap

### Track 2 — Backup / Realistic Design (priority for timeline)
- Standard PET / HDPE bottles (from Sorted's original presentation)
- Easily sourceable, readily available colors, no custom molds, quick procurement
- Can be upgraded 6-12 months post-launch without brand disruption
- **Rationale:** ensures June 1 market launch regardless of Track 1 sourcing challenges

## Why both tracks (risk model)

| Risk | Track 1 only | Track 2 only | BOTH |
|---|---|---|---|
| June 1 launch slips due to vendor delay | HIGH | LOW | LOW |
| Premium brand positioning compromised at launch | LOW | MEDIUM | LOW (hybrid: aim Track 1, fall back Track 2) |
| Cost overrun | HIGH | LOW | MEDIUM |

The two-track approach lets Sorted explore Track 1 *without blocking* Track 2 production. If Track 1 lands by April 10 design lock, great — use it. If not, ship Track 2 and upgrade post-launch.

## Updated by Patco discovery (March 23)

After [[patco-pharmaceuticals]] confirmed they can customize both bottle AND cap, the strategy evolved:

> [[binita]]: *"Since we have found vendor who can customize bottle, can we rule out standard neck bottle and stick to cylinder + rectangle bottles?"*
>
> Shivendra: *"Yesss! We can. We do have a free hand based on what we've spoken to the manufacturer. They just need detailed renders to execute."*

So as of 2026-03-23, Track 1 (aspirational) is **the primary path** with Patco as the executor. Track 2 remains as a documented fallback.

## Deliverable specs

2. Premium exploration option — square/rectangular + cylindrical with flush cap (Track 1)

Each includes: front of pack, back of pack, wraparound design for cylinders.
