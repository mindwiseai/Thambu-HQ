## The 11 points

### Updates needed

1. **Slide 2 — bottle dimensions need updating.** Manufacturer ([[patco-pharmaceuticals]]) confirmed 100ml rectangle bottle is **48×48mm × 80mm height**. Deck still shows older 7.6cm × 4.2cm. Outer packaging dimensions (slide 3) need recalculating.

2. **[[csir-cdri-logo-requirement|CSIR-CDRI logo]]** — must be on the bottle (credit requirement from CDRI). Can be in any form — small insignia, side panel, near bottom — but must be in the brief.

3. **[[blister-pack-specs|Blister packs]]** — deck only covers bottle + outer box, but blister pack specs needed too. Two dimensions previously shared in chat: 60-bottle (70_10_120) and 120-bottle (115_75_125).

4. **[[tic-tac-seal-concept|Tic Tac partial-opening seal]]** — design exploration, pending manufacturer confirmation. Should be in the brief so it's on everyone's radar.

### Suggestions

5. **Slide 11 — bacosides differentiation** — current copy says clinical gold standard is 50% bacosides. Sharper version: *"Most Bacopa supplements contain only 20-30% bacosides. Mindwise delivers a clinically optimized 55%."* See [[55-percent-bacosides]] and [[placebo-wellness-enemy]].

6. **Slide 16 — Sodium value check** — shows 0.81g, seems high for a single capsule. Likely unit error (0.81mg?). **REGULATORY RISK** if shipped as-is.

7. **Slide 17 — QR code destination** — what should it link to? Website still in dev (see [[website-development]]). Decide: product info page, Amazon listing as interim, or Linktree-style landing.

8. **Slide on dosage** — direction says "1 capsule, 2 times daily" but for **children 7+ it's 1 capsule per day**. Since students are core audience ([[four-cohorts]]), add age-based dosage note. (See [[60-capsule-bottle-decision]] for the LTV implication.)

9. **Slide 10 — "Cruelty free"** — more common in cosmetics than supplements in India. "Vegan" already covers animal-free angle. Cleaner to drop and keep claims category-appropriate.

10. **"Made in India" callout** — consider adding subtle flag insignia. Ties into Mindwise positioning as government-backed Indian supplement with fully Indian supply chain.

### Minor / structural

11. **Slides 15 and 18 redundancy** — both say "Same as bottle." Could simplify by stating once and only showing slides that differ for outer packaging. Tighter deck for whoever's executing.
