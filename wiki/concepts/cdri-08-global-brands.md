# CDRI-08 Global Brand Map

[[cdri-08]] has been commercialized globally under multiple brand names across different markets. This is documented in [[shivendra-selvam|Shivendra]]'s outreach template (Nov 17, 2025 research notes) and the Jan 14, 2026 KeenMind research.

## Brand names by market

| Brand | Market(s) | Status |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| **KeenMind** | Australia, South Africa, parts of Middle East | SFI agreement expired Dec 2024 ([[keenmind-agreement-expiry]]); potential new buyer (see [[pier-campanini]]) |
| **MemoPlus Gold** | Philippines | Legacy — 20+ years, 15L+ capsules sold (per [[brand-brief-jan-2026]]) |
| **Memory Perfect** | Europe | Distributed via [[sfi-health]] network |
| **MindWise** | India | Current — [[lumen-marketing-company]] |
| **Actif Mind** | Thailand | Status unknown |

Additionally:
- **Glykon** — distributed by Valentia Biologics S.L. in Spain
- Other brands may exist in Germany, Italy, UK, Portugal via the [[sfi-health|SFI distributor network]]

## Shivendra's outreach template

From the Nov 17, 2025 Bacopa research page, [[shivendra-selvam|Shivendra]]'s standard outreach pitch:

> "Lumen Life Sciences, official licensed partner of Central Drug Research Institute, India (CDRI). Bacopa CDRI-08, a proprietary extract with over 40 years of research behind it. Multiple gold-standard human clinical trials in cognitive health."

Claimed benefits in the template:
- Maintaining healthy cognitive function
- Improving working memory
- Supporting learning retention
- Enhancing concentration and focus
- Assisting during periods of stress or mild anxiety

## Other global Bacopa products referenced

From the research notes:
- **Vitamin Express** — "Brain Power" product link
- **Vegavero** (Germany) — Brahmi tablets, organic
- **Moringa + Bacopa** combination — via Sai Brother

## Strategic significance

The global brand map shows that [[cdri-08]] has proven commercial viability across 5+ markets. [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] is the India play, but the broader Lumen strategy (via [[indus-life-sciences|Indus]] and international outreach) could eventually extend to recapturing markets where SFI agreements have lapsed.
