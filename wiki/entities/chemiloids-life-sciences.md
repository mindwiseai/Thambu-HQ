# Chemiloids Life Sciences Pvt Ltd

Vijayawada-based (Andhra Pradesh) life sciences company that signed an NDA with [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen Marketing Company]] on **April 24, 2023** to collaborate on processing a CSIR-CDRI licensed Terminalia chebula extract.

## Key details

- **Registered address:** 40-15-14, Brindavan Colony, Vijayawada - 520010, Andhra Pradesh
- **CEO:** Kiran Bhupathiraju
- **Business purpose:** Processing of a standardized fraction enriched with N-012-0001 for the management of **Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH)**, developed by [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]]
- **The extract:** Naturally occurring compound from the Indian medicinal plant **Terminalia chebula**
- **CDRI license:** Licensed to Lumen vide agreement dated **February 17, 2019**
- **NDA jurisdiction:** Courts at Vijayawada
- **NDA term:** 1 year from effective date, with 10-year confidentiality survival

## Significance for Lumen

This NDA reveals that [[lumen-marketing-company|LMC]] holds **a second CSIR-CDRI license** beyond the [[cdri-08]] Bacopa monnieri extract. The Terminalia chebula / BPH product represents a potential **second product line** entirely separate from Mindwise's cognitive wellness positioning.

> [!note]
> The NDA in the repo is titled "NDA Chebula" — "Chebula" refers to Terminalia chebula, the plant species, not the company name. The company is Chemiloids Life Sciences. An earlier draft version ("NDA Chebula draft") also exists in the repo.
