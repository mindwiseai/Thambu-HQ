# Indus Life Sciences

Partnership entity led by **Vikash** and **Mani**, explored in a December 13, 2025 meeting with [[shivendra-selvam|Shivendra]] and [[thambu]]. The strategic frame: **LMC x Indus = all things related to Bacopa in formulation and variations, delivered by LMC, with Indus taking products to international markets.**

> [!note]
> This entity was previously referenced as "Indus Life Sciences (Chennai)" in [[product-range-expansion]] with an NDA for development of gummies, tablets, capsules, and oral liquids. This OneNote meeting (Dec 13, 2025) provides the origin story and partnership terms.

## Partnership terms (Dec 13, 2025)

- [[lumen-marketing-company|LMC]] x Indus = all Bacopa formulation and variation work delivered by LMC
- Indus takes products to international markets
- **Philippines completely excluded for Indus** — LMC already operates there with MemoPlus Gold (see [[southeast-asia-exclusion]])
- LMC to provide all material and success stories of what made the product click in Philippines in return
- [[shivendra-selvam|Shivendra]] (referred to as "Shivendra – Indus (Vikash)") to establish the working relationship
