# MindCaps — Trademark-able Term Proposal

Proposed by [[shivendra-selvam]] on **January 30, 2026** (post-[[2026-01-late-meeting-4-brand-identity|Meeting 4 — Brand Identity]]):

> "How about calling ours - 'MindCaps' instead of just capsules? Serving is 2 MindCaps a day"

## Rationale

- Creates a branded, trademark-able term for the capsule format
- Differentiates from generic "capsules" language
- Aligns with the [[positioning-shift|performance positioning]] — sounds tech/modern, not clinical
- Could reinforce daily habit framing ("Take your MindCaps")

## Current status

**Not adopted as of April 2026.** The term hasn't appeared in any subsequent meeting notes, packaging briefs, or marketing materials. It may have been informally set aside or simply not picked up by [[sorted-agency]].

## Potential value

If the [[product-range-expansion|product range expands]] to gummies and syrups, having a branded capsule term could help differentiate the capsule SKU. Worth revisiting when the range architecture is formalized.
