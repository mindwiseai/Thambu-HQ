# Semi-Transparent Bottle

The hero design decision for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] packaging. Locked in [[2026-01-late-meeting-4-brand-identity|Meeting 4]] (late January 2026). **The single design choice that solves the [[scary-drug-problem]] in one move.**

## The insight

The green CDRI-08 capsules inside the bottle are *visually distinctive* — bright, poppy, eye-catching. The previous packaging hid them inside a white opaque bottle, treating the product like something to be concealed (medicinal aesthetic). The new packaging **shows** them, treating the product like something to be celebrated.

This is consistent with high-end performance brands (AG1, Heights, Whoop, Über) that lean into product-as-design.

| Bottle size | 100mL — **now shorter than original** per April 10 Patco finalization (exact H/W proportions pending cap height confirmation). Earlier spec of 48×48mm × 80mm height (from [[2026-03-23-binita-packaging-feedback]]) is superseded. |

This forces a label-layout redesign: font size increases, text tightening, and potentially a full re-do of all current mockups on the new geometry. See [[2026-04-10-to-11-sorted-whatsapp-packaging-push]].

## Why this solves so many problems at once

1. **Solves [[scary-drug-problem]]:** transparent shows nothing to hide; minimal copy avoids prescription feel
2. **Solves [[age-paradox]]:** modern enough for 22-year-olds, sophisticated enough for 65-year-olds
3. **Solves "looks like every other Bacopa":** none of the [[placebo-wellness-enemy|generic Bacopa]] competitors do this
4. **Solves performance ad photography:** clean visuals shoot beautifully
5. **Solves the [[your-unfair-advantage]] proof:** the bottle visibly *contains* something distinctive — the proof is the product itself

## Manik's logic in Meeting 4

> "Your green capsules are poppy/eye-catching. They should be visible/celebrated, not hidden. Semi-transparent bottle reveals the visual differentiator. Simple execution: transparent bottle + frosted sleeve + green showing through. Not complicated to produce. Creates natural intrigue: 'What is this brand?' curiosity."

## Two-track sourcing

The realization that semi-transparent + custom shape might be hard to source domestically led to the [[two-track-bottle-strategy]]:
- **Track 1:** Aspirational custom (square / rectangular / cylindrical with flush cap, premium vendors)
- **Track 2:** Realistic backup (standard PET/HDPE bottle, easily sourced — protects June 1 launch)

[[patco-pharmaceuticals|Patco]] showed up in March and resolved this by being able to customize both tracks.

## Risks (per [[binita]]'s March 20 observation)

> "Concern: Contrast between minimal premium bottle + info-heavy outer box may dilute bold, premium brand imagery on shelf. Risk: Brand may slip into 'clinical/medicinal space' instead of 'sharp, everyday performance enhancer.'"

In other words: the bottle is solved but the **outer box** must carry the same minimal aesthetic. The [[2026-03-23-binita-packaging-feedback|March 23 feedback]] addresses some of this.
