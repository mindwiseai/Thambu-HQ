# Instagram Growth Tactics

The plan to take RaceSims Instagram (@race.sims) from **537 → 10,000+ followers within 6 months**, closing the gap with [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]] (~8,115). Sourced from the [[2026-03-25-vrh-battle-plan|VRH Battle Plan]].

## Posting cadence

**5 posts per week** across the content pillars below.

## Five content pillars (post these in rotation)

### 1. Product Showcase Reels
- Unboxing, setup, first-drive reaction videos
- Reels get 3-5× more reach than static posts
- Showcase the premium [[conspit|Conspit]] / [[vnm|VNM]] / [[moza|Moza]] product lines

### 2. Race Engineer Insights ⭐
- **The USP-defining pillar.** See [[race-engineer-positioning]].
- Telemetry comparisons, setup tips, "what real drivers use"
- VRH literally cannot copy this
- Examples: "Why I'd choose hydraulic pedals", "Telemetry breakdown of last weekend's setup", "Real vs sim braking curve"

### 3. Customer Setup Features
- "Setup of the Week" series
- Tag customers → they reshare → free reach
- Builds community + social proof

### 4. Sim vs Real
- Side-by-side comparisons of sim racing vs real track footage
- **Viral format** — high share probability
- Uses [[race-engineer-positioning|race engineer credibility]] to make comparisons authoritative

### 5. Behind the Scenes
- Warehouse, shipping, testing new products
- Builds trust + relatability
- Humanizes the brand

## Growth tactics

### Influencer collaborations
- **[[jamie-shaw-racing|Jamie Shaw Racing]]** — already collabs with VRH, audience overlap
- Indian sim racers with 1K-10K followers (micro-influencers)
- Send product for content
- Pitch the race-engineer angle as differentiation

### Monthly giveaways
- **"Follow + Tag 3 friends to win a Moza wheel"**
- Costs: one product
- Gains: 500-1,000 followers per giveaway

### Instagram ads
- ₹500-1000/day budget
- **Targeting:** males 18-40 in India interested in F1, racing games, sim racing
- **Boost the best Reels** (don't run cold creative — boost what's already performing)

### Aggressive engagement
- Comment on every Indian motorsport post
- Reply to every comment on RaceSims posts
- DM new followers with welcome + discount code

### Hashtag strategy
`#SimRacingIndia` `#RacingSimulator` `#MozaRacing` `#SimRig` `#F1Gaming` `#IndianMotorsport` `#RaceSims`

## Content multiplier (with [[blog-content-pipeline]])

Every blog article should produce 3-5 Instagram carousels:
- Key takeaways carousel
- Tip-format carousel
- Comparison-table carousel
- Race-engineer commentary clip
- "Read the full guide" CTA carousel

This makes the [[blog-content-pipeline|10-article SEO pipeline]] also a 30-50 piece Instagram pipeline. Same effort, two channels.
