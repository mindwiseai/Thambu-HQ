## Duplicate situation

Significant duplication across directories:
- `BACOPA REPORTS/` and `BACOPA REPORTS 2/` are identical
- `Bacopa data dump/` mostly duplicates `Bacopa articles, study and trials/`
- Several research PDFs appear in 3+ locations

## Ingestion priority

These papers back the marketing claims in [[cdri-08]] and should be individually ingested if any new clinical claims are needed. The existing wiki trial pages ([[stough-2008-90-day-trial]], [[roodenrys-2002-retention-trial]], etc.) cover the major ones.
