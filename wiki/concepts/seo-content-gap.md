# SEO Content Gap (vs VRH)

The biggest single deficit RaceSims has against [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]] in search visibility. Diagnosed in the [[2026-03-25-vrh-battle-plan|VRH Battle Plan]].

## The gap in one keyword

> **"best sim racing cockpit India"** — VRH ranks #1 with a blog guide. RaceSims doesn't rank at all.

This is a **buyer-stage** keyword. Someone searching it has decided to buy and is comparing options. VRH owns the entire research-to-purchase funnel for this query.

## Where each ranks (Google India)

| Keyword | Buyer intent | RaceSims | VRH |
|---|---|---|---|
| sim racing India | Awareness | #1-2 ✅ | #3-4 |
| racing simulator India | Awareness | #1 ✅ | #3 |
| **best sim racing cockpit India** | **Buyer** | **NOT RANKING** ❌ | **#1 (blog)** |
| moza racing India | Brand-specific | Lower | #1-2 |
| sim racing equipment buy India | Buyer | #2-3 | #1 |

RaceSims wins on broad/awareness terms (likely brand strength). VRH wins on **commercial intent** terms.

## Why this matters

Awareness traffic is interesting; **commercial-intent traffic is revenue.** VRH owns 4 of the top 5 commercial-intent terms. Even if RaceSims has more brand recall, VRH gets the buyer at the moment of decision.

## The fix — [[blog-content-pipeline|10-article SEO content pipeline]]

The VRH battle plan prescribes 10 high-intent blog articles to write:

1. **"Best Sim Racing Setup in India 2026: Complete Buyer's Guide"** — direct attack on the master keyword
2. "Moza R5 vs Moza R9 vs Moza R12: Which to Buy in India?"
3. "Conspit vs Other Premium Pedals: Why Hydraulic Pedals Are Worth It in India"
4. "Best Sim Racing Pedals India: Hydraulic vs Load Cell vs Potentiometer"
5. **"How Sim Racing Helps Real Racing Drivers: A Race Engineer's Perspective"** — VRH cannot write this (see [[race-engineer-positioning]])
6. "Complete Sim Racing Rig Setup Guide for Indian Homes"
7. **"Conspit vs VNM Cockpits: In-Depth Review from a Race Engineer"** — race-engineer voice
8. "Sim Racing for Aspiring Indian Racing Drivers: Training Guide"
9. "Best Budget Sim Racing Setup Under 50,000 INR" — entry-level capture
10. "Direct Drive Wheel Bases Explained: What Indian Sim Racers Need to Know"

Each article should:
- Target the keyword in title + H1 + URL
- Have FAQ section + FAQPage schema (highest AI citation rate per [[geo-content-readiness]])
- Be 1,500–3,000 words for SEO depth
- Include comparison tables, internal links to product pages
- Use [[race-engineer-positioning|race-engineer voice]] where authentic
