📋 Executive Summary

Overall Assessment

RaceSims.in has a solid traditional SEO foundation — excellent Core Web Vitals, good product schema markup, rich product descriptions with FAQ sections, and a valid sitemap with 122 discovered pages. However, there are several on-page SEO issues (duplicate H1 tags, duplicate Organization schemas, inconsistent brand naming) and a critical gap in AI/Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) — the site currently has zero AI crawler rules, missing the rapidly growing AI-driven search traffic channel. AI-driven traffic to Shopify stores grew 8x year-over-year in 2025, and AI-driven orders grew 15x.

🤖 PART 2: Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) Audit

Why GEO Matters for RaceSims

Generative Engine Optimization is about making your content visible to AI search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Claude. When a customer asks ChatGPT "What's the best direct drive wheelbase available in India?", you want RaceSims products recommended. AI-driven traffic to Shopify grew 8x YoY in 2025, and AI-driven orders grew 15x. FAQ schema content has one of the highest citation rates (10.5%) in AI-generated answers.

🎯 PART 3: Prioritized Recommendations

🔴 Critical Priority (Do First)

1. Add AI Crawler Rules to robots.txt

Add explicit allow rules for AI search bots in your Shopify robots.txt. This is especially critical for OAI-SearchBot (ChatGPT Shopping) which fetches live product data for recommendations. In Shopify, go to Settings → Custom Data → robots.txt and add rules.

User-agent: GPTBot Allow: / User-agent: OAI-SearchBot Allow: / User-agent: ChatGPT-User Allow: / User-agent: Google-Extended Allow: / User-agent: ClaudeBot Allow: / User-agent: anthropic-ai Allow: / User-agent: PerplexityBot Allow: / User-agent: CCBot Allow: /
📈 Impact: Enables AI-driven product discovery. ChatGPT Shopping alone could drive high-intent traffic. Effort: Low (15 min)

2. Fix Duplicate H1 Tags (All Pages)

Every page has 2-3 H1 tags due to Shopify theme. The theme's header__heading class outputs an empty H1, and product pages have both a page H1 and product title H1. Edit the theme to ensure exactly one H1 per page. Change the logo/header to a <div> or <span> and keep only the product name as H1.

📈 Impact: Improves heading hierarchy clarity for search engines and AI crawlers. Effort: Medium (theme edit)

3. Fix Duplicate Organization Schemas & Brand Naming

Every page has two Organization schemas with different names: "Race Sims" and "RaceSims solutions Pvt Ltd". Remove one and standardize to a single brand name across all schemas, title tags, and content. Suggested: "RaceSims" as the brand name, with "RaceSims Solutions Pvt Ltd" only as the legal entity name in the Organization schema.

📈 Impact: Establishes consistent brand entity for both Google Knowledge Graph and AI model recognition. Effort: Medium

4. Fix 51 Pages Returning 404 Errors

Review the 51 URLs returning 404 in Google Search Console. Set up proper 301 redirects for any that had traffic, or ensure they are removed from internal links and sitemap. Go to GSC → Indexing → Pages → "Not found (404)" to see the full list.

📈 Impact: Recovers lost link equity, improves crawl budget efficiency. Effort: Medium

🟡 High Priority (Do Soon)

5. Add BreadcrumbList Schema

Add BreadcrumbList JSON-LD schema to all product and collection pages. This helps Google display breadcrumb rich results and helps AI models understand your site hierarchy (Home → Category → Product).

{ "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "BreadcrumbList", "itemListElement": [ {"@type": "ListItem", "position": 1, "name": "Home", "item": "https://racesims.in/"}, {"@type": "ListItem", "position": 2, "name": "Wheelbases", "item": "https://racesims.in/collections/wheelbases"}, {"@type": "ListItem", "position": 3, "name": "VNM Direct Drive Elite"} ] }
📈 Impact: Enables breadcrumb rich results in Google + better AI content parsing. Effort: Medium

6. Add Product Review/Rating Schema

Implement a product review system (e.g., Judge.me, Loox, or Shopify's native reviews) and add AggregateRating and Review schema to Product JSON-LD. AI models heavily favor products with visible social proof when making recommendations.

📈 Impact: Star ratings in search results + higher AI recommendation probability. Effort: Medium

7. Shorten and Optimize Title Tags

Current titles are 80-95 chars (Google truncates at ~60). Remove " – RaceSims solutions Pvt Ltd" suffix and keep it concise. Example: "Conspit 290GP Formula Racing Wheel | Carbon Fiber | RaceSims India" (66 chars).

📈 Impact: Better click-through rates in search results. Effort: Low

8. Fix Missing Alt Text on Images

Multiple product pages have images without alt text (4 on homepage, 5 on VNM Elite page). Add descriptive alt text that includes product name and feature. E.g., "VNM Direct Drive Elite wheelbase CNC aluminum housing side view".

📈 Impact: Improves image SEO, accessibility, and AI content understanding. Effort: Low

🟢 Medium Priority (Plan for Next Month)

9. Create Buying Guide / Comparison Blog Content

AI models cite comparison articles at very high rates. Create authoritative content like:

  • "Best Direct Drive Wheelbases in India 2026 — Complete Buying Guide"
  • "Conspit vs VNM: Which Sim Racing Ecosystem is Right for You?"
  • "How to Build a Complete Sim Racing Setup in India — Beginner to Pro"
  • "Formula Wheels vs GT Wheels: Which Should You Choose?"
📈 Impact: Major GEO opportunity — AI models cite guides/comparisons 3-5x more than product pages. Effort: High (content creation)

10. Add LocalBusiness Schema

Add LocalBusiness or Store schema with your Chennai office address, phone, email, operating hours, and geo-coordinates. This helps with "sim racing near me" and India-specific AI queries.

{ "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "Store", "name": "RaceSims", "address": { "@type": "PostalAddress", "addressLocality": "Chennai", "addressRegion": "Tamil Nadu", "addressCountry": "IN" }, "telephone": "+91-XXXXXXXXXX", "url": "https://racesims.in" }
📈 Impact: Local search and AI local recommendation visibility. Effort: Low

11. Strengthen About Us / Authority Page

Create or enhance an About page that emphasizes: authorized Conspit & VNM distributor status, authorized service partner credentials, years in business, team expertise, and customer base. This builds E-E-A-T signals that both Google and AI models use to assess authority.

📈 Impact: Builds brand entity authority for knowledge graphs and AI trust scoring. Effort: Medium

12. Review "Crawled - Currently Not Indexed" Pages (63)

63 pages were crawled by Google but not indexed — meaning Google found them but didn't deem them worth indexing. These could be thin content pages, filter/variant URLs, or low-value pages. Review and either improve content, consolidate, or noindex them.

📈 Impact: Improves overall site quality signals and crawl budget. Effort: Medium-High
