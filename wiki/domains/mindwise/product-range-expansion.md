# Product Range Expansion

Confirmed by [[shivendra-selvam]] on **March 20, 2026** in response to [[binita]]'s question about future-proofing the packaging system:

> "Thinking ahead - yes. We are working on creating a range. Bacopa for children, Bacopa + other elements and Bacopa in syrup form."

## Gummy formulation details (from Technical Catalog)

Per the "Calculation of Bacosides in Gummies" documents:
- Each gummy: 4500mg total weight
- Contains 350mg BacoZest (17% bacosides = ~59mg bacosides/gummy)
- Recovery rate: 75.98–94.17% (considered acceptable)
- Uses BacoZest from Chemiloids (alternative to CDRI-08 extract)

> [!note]
> The gummy formulation uses **BacoZest** (Chemiloids' Bacopa extract at 17% bacosides), NOT the [[cdri-08|CDRI-08]] extract (55% bacosides). This is a different ingredient with significantly lower bacoside concentration. Marketing claims about CDRI-08 cannot be applied to the gummy format unless the formulation changes.

## Packaging implications

[[binita]]'s insight (Mar 20): the packaging system needs to be **future-proof** — designed so new products can slot into the same visual language without a full redesign. This influenced the decision to keep the [[route-3-architecture-of-intelligence|Route 3 logo]] and [[monochrome-plus-green-pop|color system]] modular.
