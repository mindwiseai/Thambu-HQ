## The proposal

[[sorted-agency|Sorted]] submitted a website proposal: **₹6,00,000** for a custom Shopify store.

**Scope included:**
- UI/UX design
- Shopify development
- Razorpay integration
- Email capture (Klaviyo)
- Analytics setup (GA + Meta Pixel)
- Review integration
- Full website copy written by Sorted

**Timeline:** 10–15 days design + 30–35 days development.

**Pages:** Homepage, Product page (optimized for conversion), About Us, Add to Cart, Checkout (~5–7 pages total).

## The budget gap
