# Packaging Content Layout — 4-Side Bottle + Outer Box

The four-sided bottle label layout was **finalized in the April 8, 2026 call** between [[thambu]], [[ankita]], and [[shivendra-selvam]]. This replaces the earlier Sorted-provided layout which had content issues.

## 4-Side Bottle Layout

### Side 1 — Front (Hero)
- **Mindwise** logo
- **"Your Unfair Advantage"** ([[your-unfair-advantage|tagline]])
- **"India's only government-backed brain food supplement"** (note: "food" was added per [[ankita]]'s suggestion — avoid positioning as clinical/pharmaceutical)
- **60 capsules**
- **Vegetarian symbol** (green dot)
- **Nothing else.** Keep it clean. Apple-product philosophy: not one extra element.

### Side 2 — Science
- **"Not all Bacopa is created equal"** (header — retained from Sorted's original)
- Paragraph on why this Bacopa is special:
  - 40+ years of research
  - 7+ gold standard clinical trials
  - **Optimal blend** of bacosides (do NOT mention 55% — see [[cdri-55-brand-name]])
  - Developed and standardized by [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]]
  - "Purity and precision are essential for brain performance" (closer)
- Remove all bacoside percentage numbers
- Remove generic CDRI-08 boilerplate

### Side 3 — Credibility
- **CSIR-CDRI logo** (top) — see [[csir-cdri-logo-requirement]]
- **"Clinically proven [[cdri-55-brand-name|CDRI-55]]"** badge (center)
- **QR code** (links to product/clinical info)
- **GMP certified, allergen-free, gluten-free** icons
- "Refer to outer packaging for detailed information" (AceBlend reference)

### Side 4 — Benefits
- 4-quadrant benefit box (similar to existing Mindwise bottle)
- Benefits to be refined from KeenMind Australia pamphlets (Thambu shared during call)
- Benefits wording: align with new brand strategy, not clinical language

## Content decisions from April 8 call

| Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|
| Add "food" to "brain supplement" → "brain food supplement" | [[ankita]]: avoid pharmaceutical perception, position as daily wellness |
| Remove "cruelty free" | Product has undergone animal testing ([[cdri-08-safety-profile|toxicity studies]] on rats, rabbits, rhesus monkeys) |
| Remove "vegan" — use "vegetarian capsules" | HPMC capsules are vegetarian; "vegan" claim needs separate certification |
| Do NOT say "1 month supply" | [[thambu]]: regulatory concern in India; only "serving size: 2 capsules" allowed in dosage section |
| Keep front minimal | [[thambu]]: "The idea of us adding an exterior dabba is so that we have the nicest looking bottle... like an Apple product. Not one extra speaker." |
| Use "optimum" not "55%" | [[thambu]]/[[cdri-55-brand-name]]: CDRI scientists themselves advised against advertising the number |

## Outer box (exterior packaging)

- **Front:** Same as bottle front (hero)
- **One side:** Nutritional information, direction of use, dosage — all regulatory requirements
- **Two sides:** Available for additional content — pamphlet-style info from KeenMind Australia materials
- Design philosophy: **All regulatory clutter on the box, keep the bottle premium-clean**
- Box status: still under [[outer-cardboard-box-decision|decision]] — may be dropped at launch
