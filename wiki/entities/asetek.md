# Asetek SimSports

Premium sim-racing hardware manufacturer (Denmark). [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] has a **reseller relationship** with tiered pricing based on annual order volume. NDA signed. Pricelist dated September 2024.

## Product lines

Three tiers:
1. **Invicta** (flagship) — wheelbase $1,299.99, pedals $759-799, formula button box $1,249.99
2. **Forte** (mid-range) — wheelbase $899.99, pedals $459-499, formula wheel $544.99
3. **La Prima** (entry) — wheelbase $679.99, pedals $334.99, formula wheel $334.99

## Pricing tiers (reseller)

| Tier | Annual order value | Discount range |
|------|-------------------|---------------|
| Tier 1 | <$25,000 | ~10% off MSRP |
| Tier 2 | $25,000-$99,999 | 20-39% off MSRP |
| Tier 3 | $100,000-$249,999 | Higher |
| Tier 4 | $250,000-$499,999 | Higher |
| Tier 5 | $500,000+ | Best pricing |

**Best margins at Tier 2:** Forte Formula Wheel at 39.1%, Invicta Formula Button Box at 30%, Invicta Wheelbase at 27.4%.
