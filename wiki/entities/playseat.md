# Playseat

Dutch sim-racing cockpit brand — one of the most recognised names in sim-racing seats/frames globally. Accessible to [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] in India through the **[[techxhub]]** Authorized Retailer Partner (ARP) agreement — not imported directly.

## Strategic role in RaceSims portfolio

Playseat provides a **branded cockpit alternative** to RaceSims' own GT-rig frames. Customers who want a known international brand name on the frame (rather than a custom RaceSims-built cockpit) can now be served without RaceSims having to build a competing frame line.

Playseat products accessible via Techxhub (per March 2026 pricelist):
- Seat + frame cockpits (various tiers)
- Chassis accessories

See [[techxhub]] for the full Playseat range INR pricing.

## Trade-off vs. RaceSims in-house frames

- **Playseat pro:** instant brand recognition, globally marketed
- **RaceSims frame pro:** better margin, full customization, domestic manufacturing story
- Both can coexist — different customer segments
