CTDDR-2025

Innovation for Tomorrow's Cure

CTDDR-2025 is a drug discovery and development conference focused on cutting-edge research in infectious diseases, metabolic, endocrine and neurodegenerative disorders and cancer. The conference will also showcase advances in medicinal and natural product chemistry that are enabling breakthroughs in drug discovery.

  • Emerging technologies to enable drug discovery
  • Concept to point-of-care: Drugs recently approved
  • Advances and challenges in drug discovery for parasitic diseases
  • Tackling anti-microbial resistance
  • Natural product chemistry for novel drugs
  • New approaches in anti-viral drug discovery and development
  • Emerging therapeutics for CNS and CVS disorders
  • New strategies in synthetic and medicinal chemistry
  • Novel targets in bone and reproductive health
  • Anti-cancer therapeutics and prevention of relapse
