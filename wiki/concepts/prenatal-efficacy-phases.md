# Prenatal Efficacy — Phased Clinical Proof

Four-phase approach to building clinical evidence for the [[domains/prenatal/_index|prenatal brand]]. Modeled on [[ritual-benchmark|Ritual's]] playbook.

## Phase 1 — Launch with published science (Day 1)

Published peer-reviewed literature on each ingredient (L-Methylfolate, DHA, chelated iron, etc.). Sufficient for [[prenatal-fsdu-regulatory|FSSAI compliance]].

## Phase 2 — Observational study (6-12 months post-launch)

Partner with hospital gynecologists to track outcomes in women already taking the product. Lower ethics burden than interventional trial.

## Phase 3 — Comparative clinical study (Year 2-3)

Head-to-head study: prenatal brand product vs standard Indian prenatal. Measure blood biomarkers. Partner with AIIMS or top medical college. This becomes the biggest marketing asset (Ritual's playbook).

## Phase 4 — Additional trust signals (ongoing)

- Clean Label Project certification
- Third-party heavy metal testing every batch
- USP/NSF verification

## Note on pregnant women in research

- Classified as **vulnerable population** under ICMR National Ethical Guidelines
- Full ethics committee review required (not expedited)
- Supplement comparison studies (not drug trials) with minimal fetal risk can qualify for approval
