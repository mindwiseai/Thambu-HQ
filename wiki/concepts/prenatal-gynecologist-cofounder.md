# Gynecologist Co-Founder Strategy

The [[domains/prenatal/_index|prenatal brand]] requires a gynecologist at **co-founder level** — not advisor, not consultant, not endorser. This is Step 0 on the [[prenatal-launch-roadmap|launch roadmap]] and blocks everything else.

## Why co-founder, not advisor

- Drives formulation decisions (not just rubber-stamps)
- Provides clinical credibility for [[prenatal-fsdu-regulatory|FSSAI claims]]
- Regulatory strength — a practicing OB-GYN co-founding adds weight to FSDU applications
- Marketing asset — face of the brand in content
- Differentiator from [[trimacare]] (which uses doctor prescribing, not doctor founding)

## Ideal profile

- Practicing OB-GYN (not retired)
- Public presence: Instagram or YouTube educating women on pregnancy health
- Willing to be brand co-founder, not just paid endorser
- Based in a metro city (for accessibility)

## Critical positioning rule

> The gynecologist adds credibility to the brand; the brand doesn't become the gynecologist.

Position as "science-led company with medical professionals" — NOT "Dr. So-and-so's vitamin."
