# Beta-Free (Bitter-Free) Bacopa Extract

A **bitter-removed form of Bacopa monnieri extract** surfaced in the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]] as a strategic unlock for expanding [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] beyond capsules into **gummies and syrups**.

> [!note]
> The voice brief says "beta-free" — this is almost certainly a speech-to-text artifact of "**bitter-free**". Standard Bacopa extract is notably bitter (medicinal / vegetal taste), which is why existing supplements use capsules that mask the taste.

## The unlock

Standard [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 Bacopa extract]] is **too bitter** for direct oral formats (chews, gummies, syrups). Capsules work because the coating hides the taste from the tongue. But capsules limit:

- **Kids** (can't swallow capsules)
- **Elderly** (dysphagia common)
- **Convenience users** (gummies are preferred for daily habit adherence)
- **Markets with gummy preference** (US, Philippines, parts of SEA)

A bitter-free Bacopa extract would let Mindwise produce:
- **Bacopa gummies** — children, elderly, convenience seekers
- **Bacopa syrups** — pediatric + elderly + traditional Ayurvedic format users

## The supply chain

Per the voice brief:

```
IMCD (Mumbai supplier) — has the bitter-free Bacopa
       ↓
Solusta (Puducherry manufacturer) — can produce gummies
       ↓
Corporate client (already interested, waiting for bitter-free)
+ Philippines market (existing MemoPlus Gold market, asking for gummies)
       ↓
Lumen Marketing Company — potential new product line
```

Two real demand signals:
1. **Solusta already has a corporate client** interested in Bacopa gummies, blocked by bitterness
2. **Philippines market** (the [[adam-ritson|historic MemoPlus Gold territory]]) is asking for the gummy format

## Relationship to the June 1 capsule launch

**This does NOT compete with the [[launch-plan-june-1-2026|June 1 capsule launch]]**. The capsule launch stays on track and is the flagship SKU. Gummy / syrup expansion is **additive** — a separate product line developed on its own timeline, targeting different buyer contexts.

The strategic framing:
- **Capsules** → premium adult cognitive wellness daily habit ([[your-unfair-advantage]])
- **Gummies** → kids ([[learning-is-childs-play-campaign]] content dovetails naturally), convenience users, corporate gifts
- **Syrups** → pediatric, elderly, traditional formats

## Relationship to existing CDRI-08

⚠️ **Important regulatory question:** is the bitter-free Bacopa from IMCD **the same [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 extract]]** with bitter components removed, or a **different extract** from a different manufacturer? This matters because:

- If same extract → all [[2026-ctd-module-2.5-clinical-overview|CTD clinical evidence]] still applies, [[55-percent-bacosides|55%+ bacosides]] claim still valid
- If different extract → the evidence base does not transfer. New trials needed. Can't legitimately use the same claims.

Thambu needs to confirm the supply-chain chemistry at the [[imcd|IMCD meeting]] during the [[april-2-mumbai-meeting|Mumbai trip]].
