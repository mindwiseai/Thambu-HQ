CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS (Identified Across Meetings)
================================================================================

1. PERCEPTION SHIFT EXECUTION
   The semi-transparent bottle is the hero that solves "scary clinical" feeling
   Failure point: If bottle is still cluttered with copy, perception doesn't shift

2. CROSS-AGE APPEAL
   "Unfair Advantage" + modern aesthetic must work for 18 AND 85
   Failure point: If design looks too trendy or too clinical, alienates one group

3. CONTENT-TO-CUSTOMER CONVERSION
   Educational content must ultimately lead to trial and repeat purchase
   Failure point: If sustenance content doesn't link back to product benefit

4. HEALTHCARE PROFESSIONAL TRUST
   Must build prescriber base (doctors, neurologists, psychiatrists)
   Failure point: If positioning seems "marketing-y" rather than science-backed

5. TIMING TO MARKET
   All materials locked by April, soft launch June, full by July
   Failure point: Design delays or approval bottlenecks push timeline
