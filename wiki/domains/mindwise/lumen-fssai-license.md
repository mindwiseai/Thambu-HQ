## Regulatory context

The FSSAI license is a **mandatory requirement** for selling food supplements in India. The [[csir-cdri-logo-requirement|CSIR-CDRI logo]] must also appear on packaging. Together with the FSSAI license number, these are the non-negotiable regulatory elements that must appear on every bottle and [[blister-pack-specs|blister pack]].
