# Next-Gen RaceSims Rigs

The **next generation of RaceSims cockpit / rig designs** currently in early design phase. Surfaced in the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]]. Core design direction: **cast aluminum brackets and mounting hardware** (vs. the welded-steel convention of most sim-racing rigs).

## Why cast aluminum

- **Precision** — cast aluminum allows tighter tolerances and repeatable geometry vs welded steel
- **Weight** — lighter for the same rigidity profile (important for motion systems and home transport)
- **Premium aesthetic** — consistent with the [[race-engineer-positioning|professional driver]] positioning
- **Manufacturability** — once tooling is built, production is cheaper per unit at volume
- **Differentiation** — most Indian sim-racing rigs are welded square tubing. Cast aluminum is a visible quality tier shift.

## Design governance

Thambu is keeping **design authority in-house** and treating external vendors as execution shops, not creative directors:

| Role | Who |
|---|---|
| **Functional design mentor** (brackets, mounting, geometry) | [[mr-leela-krishnan]] (ARKA Motorsports boss, real-world race team experience) |
| **Aesthetic direction / cladding** | Thambu's own renders and mockups (in the projects folder) |
| **Vendor execution** (exterior cladding) | [[shahid-moto-mind]] — but their proposed design direction was **rejected**; they become pure execution, not creative |

2. **[[arka-motorsports|Arka Motorsports]] validation loop.** Thambu is setting up a RaceSims simulator AT the Arka Motorsports facility in Coimbatore (#33B Aerodrome Road). **This is India's most decorated motorsport team — 123+ championship titles since 1987, led by [[mr-leela-krishnan|N. Leelakrishnan]], a 7-time National Rally Champion.** The next-gen rigs will be tested by real national-championship-winning drivers on real race weekends, and those drivers' feedback can be directly integrated into subsequent iterations. **This is the kind of validation VRH literally cannot access — there is only one team in India with this credential stack.**

   - The design override of [[shahid-moto-mind]]

   - Lap time correlations on the prototype vs current rigs
   - Testimonials from Arka drivers (some of whom are themselves national champions)
