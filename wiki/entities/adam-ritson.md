# Adam Ritson

Australian individual whose remarkable recovery from severe brain damage (sustained in a sports accident in the late 1990s / early 2000s) became the **catalyst that took CDRI-08 / [[cdri-08|Bacopa monnieri extract]] global**. Adam Ritson's story is the human core of the Mindwise origin narrative.

## The story (per [[2026-01-06-meeting-1-brief-discussion]] + [[2026-raw-mindwise-unique-files|full written account]])

1. Adam was a **highly promising Australian rugby league footballer** playing for **Parramatta** in the mid-1990s
2. **June 23, 1996:** At just 20 years old, Adam suffered a devastating on-field head injury
3. **Immediate consequences:** Multiple brain bleeds, emergency brain surgery, **14 surgeries over four months**, prolonged life support, extensive rehabilitation
4. **Memory damage:** Could not remember the injury, large parts of his career, struggled with short-term memory, forming new memories. Family: *"It's like he hears things for the first time, again and again."*
5. Years of intensive therapy with minimal progress — prognosis: severely affected for life
6. **1998:** Family travelled to India, visited [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen]] and [[csir-cdri|CDRI]], learned about the Bacopa extract
7. Adam began taking CDRI Bacopa extract daily as part of his routine
8. **After several weeks:** Regained ability to read, remember telephone numbers, recognize previously forgotten people, hold consistent conversations
9. Today: **Adam is a well-known rugby coach in Australia**
10. Media sensation → Olga Sortal (aunt) commercializes as "**Mimad**" brand
11. Peak sales: 100,000 packs/month in Australia
9. Product goes global as multiple sister brands:
   - **KeenMind** — Australia / South Africa / Middle East
   - **MemoPlus Gold** — Philippines (15L+ capsules sold; popular among students, ADHD, autism cases)
   - **Memory Perfect** — Europe
   - **Actif Mind** — Thailand / Malaysia
