# Solista

Puducherry-based **gummy manufacturer** that surfaced in the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]] as the key manufacturing partner for a potential **Bacopa gummy line** under Lumen / Mindwise.

## The opportunity

Per the voice brief:
> "The manufacturer for this is Solista from Puducherry. He has a corporate who's interested in Bacopa gummies, but he needs it to be bitter-free, so this will be key for that and unlock that also."

Two things are unlocking simultaneously:

1. **Solista already has a corporate client** who wants Bacopa gummies
2. **The constraint:** gummies need **bitter-free** Bacopa — which is exactly what the [[beta-free-bacopa-extract|beta-free Bacopa extract]] from [[imcd]] provides

So the full chain is:
```
IMCD (Mumbai, supplier) → beta-free Bacopa extract
     ↓
Solista (Puducherry, manufacturer) → Bacopa gummies
     ↓
Corporate client (existing demand) + Philippines market (existing demand)
     ↓
Lumen Marketing Company → new product line beyond capsules
```

## Why this matters for Mindwise

This creates a **secondary Mindwise SKU path** that's entirely separate from the June 1 capsule launch work Sorted Agency is driving. It's a supply-chain unlock that could materially expand the product roadmap in 2026-2027.

However, it does **not** compete with or replace the current launch — the Mindwise capsule launch ([[launch-plan-june-1-2026]]) stays on track for June 1. The gummy / syrup expansion is **additive**.

## Connection to Indus (Dec 13, 2025)

The [[indus-life-sciences|Indus]] meeting (Dec 13, 2025) confirms the name as **Solista**:
- [[thambu]] to speak to Solista and connect with [[shivendra-selvam|Shivendra]]
- 500g Bacopa, 500g BacoZest, 100g Chlorella to be purchased from Vikash (Indus) and **sent to Solista**
- Bacopa-based syrup samples expected as a deliverable
- This confirms Solista's role as the downstream manufacturer receiving raw materials from [[indus-life-sciences|Indus]]
