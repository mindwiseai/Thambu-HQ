## Key strategic insights

1. **Don't compete on VRH's strengths.** Don't try to match community size, retail presence, or partnership count.
2. **Compete asymmetrically.** Race-engineer voice in every piece of content. Comparison content. GEO. Reddit. Discord with #race-engineer-tips channel.
3. **First-mover Reddit + GEO.** Both untapped — get there before VRH does.
4. **The Discord unblock is 30 minutes.** The setup kit already exists. The bot already exists. Just create the server.
5. **Race-engineer voice is the moat.** Use it in every piece of content or the moat is inactive.
