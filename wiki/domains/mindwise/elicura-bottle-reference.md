# Elicura — Frosted Bottle Reference Brand

Indian brand (elicura.in) that uses the same frosted bottle design that [[thambu]] identified for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]. Referenced on **February 3, 2026** in the Sorted x Mindwise WhatsApp group.

## Context

[[thambu]] announced finding a bottle vendor and pointed to Elicura as a brand using the exact frosted bottle from that vendor. He shared a photo of the bottle. This was a key validation moment for the [[semi-transparent-bottle]] concept — proving the frosted aesthetic was achievable from an Indian supplier.

## Sorted's response

[[manik]] flagged a concern: CDRI-08 capsules are small and might not fill the entire bottle. The current bottle was 200cc; the new one from this vendor was 160cc. [[thambu]] agreed to get samples.

> [!note]
> The Elicura vendor was effectively superseded by [[patco-pharmaceuticals|Patco Pharmaceuticals]] in March 2026. Patco could customize both bottle AND cap, whereas the Elicura vendor appears to have been a standard-spec supplier.
