# Sorted Strategy Meetings 1–5 — Hub

Hub page for the **5 foundational strategy meetings** between [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen]] / [[thambu]] and [[sorted-agency|Sorted Agency]] (January 6 → February 12, 2026). In ~5 weeks, the Mindwise brand strategy went from zero to fully locked across positioning, visual identity, and content.

> **Note:** This page used to contain the full content of all 5 meetings. After the [[meta/lint-2026-04-12|first lint pass]] surfaced ~30 dead links to the individual meeting pages, the content was split into 5 atomic source pages and this page became a hub that links to them. Each individual page is now the canonical source for its meeting.

| → | From | To |

| Position | Supplement brand | Brain health platform |
