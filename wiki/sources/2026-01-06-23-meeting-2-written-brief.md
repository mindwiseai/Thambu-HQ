# Meeting 2 — Written Brief Response

**Date:** between [[2026-01-06-meeting-1-brief-discussion|Meeting 1 (Jan 6)]] and [[2026-01-23-meeting-3-brand-strategy-workshop|Meeting 3 (Jan 23)]]
**Format:** Detailed written brief response to [[sorted-agency|Sorted's]] questionnaire

## Summary

Not a live meeting — a comprehensive written response to Sorted's brief questionnaire. Established the **vision, brand story narrative, clinical references, and 3-pillar marketing strategy** that became foundational for everything that followed. Output: [[brain-health-community-vision|"Become India's most trusted brain health community"]] vision statement + clinical evidence pack.

## Key outputs

### Reinforced brand story: "Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Science"

Three-act narrative:
1. **Ancient Wisdom (3,000+ years):** Bacopa monnieri revered in Ayurveda as "medhya rasayana" (memory enhancer, mental rejuvenator)
2. **40 Years Scientific Dedication (1960s-2000s):** [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]] systematic investigation → [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 standardized extract]]
3. **Global Success Story (1990s-2020s):** Multiple brand successes (KeenMind, MemoPlus Gold, etc.) → coming home to India with Mindwise relaunch

### Singular brand proposition (interim — later evolved into [[your-unfair-advantage]])

> "The only clinically proven Bacopa-based supplement in India made with the original, clinically proven CDRI-08 extract and backed by research at CSIR-CDRI, for better memory, focus and mental clarity across ages."

Note: this is the **clinical/scientific** version of the proposition. The [[positioning-shift|clinical → performance pivot]] in Meeting 3 reframed this dramatically.

### [[brain-health-community-vision|Vision statement]] (LOCKED)

> **"Become India's Most Trusted Brain Health Community"**

This is the long-term platform vision — positions Mindwise as a brain health community/platform, not just a product brand.

### Three-pillar marketing strategy
1. **Educate** — Simplify the science (CDRI-08, trials, timelines) for layperson understanding
2. **Acquire** — Generate qualified high-intent visitors → first-time buyers at efficient CAC
3. **Retain & Build Trust** — Set correct expectations ([[8-12-week-onset-claim|8-12 week horizon]]), support adherence, turn users/doctors into advocates

### Geographic strategy
- **Phase 1 (Year 1):** Top metros — Mumbai, Delhi-NCR, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Kolkata, Ahmedabad
- **Phase 2:** Tier-2 cities; digital pan-India from Day 1

### Clinical trial references documented

- **Stough et al., 2001** — 12-week trial, visual information processing → [[stough-2001-12-week-trial]]
- **Roodenrys et al., 2002** — 40-65 age group retention (12 weeks) → [[roodenrys-2002-retention-trial]]
- **Stough et al., 2008** — 90-day RCT (107 adults), enhanced working memory → [[stough-2008-90-day-trial]]
- **Raghav et al., 2006** — Age-associated memory impairment (Indian J Psychiatry)
- **Downey et al., 2012 / Benson et al., 2013** — Acute studies, multitasking under stress

**Regulatory:** Approved by TGA (Australian FDA equivalent) — see [[2026-ctd-module-2.5-clinical-overview]]

### Doctor endorsers identified
- [[dr-dilip-ghosh]] — Director, Nutriconnect (world-renowned functional food expert)
- [[dr-hemant-singh]] — Senior Scientist, Neuro Psycho Pharmacologist (CDRI mentor, ongoing advisor)

### Community emotional outcomes (across all audiences)
Performance-based living, clarity of thought, achievement of life goals, better multitasking and focus, reduced stress and cognitive clutter, present-moment awareness, handling ambiguity.

### Current market status (as of January 2026)
- Live on Amazon "for sometime" — 25 bottles sold
- Instagram ads running
- Retention rate from other markets: very loyal (15L+ capsules sold in Philippines, famous among students, ADHD, autism cases)
- Previous India launch (Memory Sure ~2015) **failed because market wasn't ready**
