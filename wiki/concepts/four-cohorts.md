# Four Target Cohorts

The four target audiences for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]], crystallized in [[2026-01-06-meeting-1-brief-discussion|Meeting 1]] and reinforced in [[2026-02-12-meeting-5-content-strategy|Meeting 5]]. All four share the [[your-unfair-advantage|"Your Unfair Advantage"]] positioning but receive different campaign content.

## The four

### 1. Students & Exam-Goers (18-24)
- UPSC, JEE, board exams; competitive exams
- Personally or parent-motivated
- **Pain:** Long study hours, attention fatigue, stimulant abuse, exam anxiety
- **Persona:** 24-year-old competitive exam preparer; YouTube productivity content consumer; seeking non-addictive support
- **Campaign:** *Pending — not yet scripted as of Feb 12*
- **Likely angle:** "Sharper focus, better retention, the edge without the crash"

### 2. High-Pressure Professionals (25-45)
- Cognitive load, sustained performance, non-stimulant alternative
- **Pain:** Brain fog, lost confidence, decision fatigue, leadership presence eroded by mental noise
- **Persona:** 30-40 high performer; supplement-stack member; looking for focus + retention enhancement
- **Campaign:** [[lead-the-room-campaign|"Lead the Room"]] — locked, musical brand film format
- **Tagline subline:** "Sharp minds lead the room"

### 3. Proactive Agers & Caregivers (40+)
- Memory decline awareness, dementia prevention, independence
- **Pain:** Early forgetfulness, family history fear, scepticism of generic tonics
- **Persona:** 58-year-old noticing forgetfulness; skeptical of generic tonics; open to natural options with proof
- **Campaign:** *Pending — not yet scripted*
- **Likely angle:** "Stay sharp for the years ahead. Independence is the gift you give your kids."

### 4. ADHD Individuals (18+)
- Focus and cognitive stability
- **Pain:** Existing stimulant medication side effects, search for non-pharma support
- **Note:** [[cdri-08|CDRI-08]] has historical use among ADHD/autism cases via [[adam-ritson|MemoPlus Gold in the Philippines]] (15L+ capsules sold), but Mindwise CANNOT make a treatment claim
- **Campaign:** *Pending*
- **Likely angle:** Subtle — must avoid medical/treatment claim territory

## Plus: Kids (children's cognitive development)

Not strictly an "adult target" but the [[learning-is-childs-play-campaign|"Learning is Child's Play" campaign]] (Campaign 1) is about kids, marketed to parents (especially moms). This is content-marketing-shaped, not conversion-shaped — building category authority and the [[brain-health-community-vision]].

## Unifying motivation (across all)
> "I need to take this today so when I am 50-60 years old, my kids don't have to be dependent on me. I want my brain to be so healthy."

This is what makes the [[age-paradox|18-to-85 range]] possible — the motivation is the same forward-looking brain-investment frame.

## Cohort campaign rollout (per Meeting 5 staging)
1. **First:** Kids campaign (highest engagement, gentle entry point)
2. **Second:** Professionals / "Lead the Room"
3. **Third:** Aging-well campaign (TBD)
4. **Fourth:** Exam-season campaign (seasonal push, tied to Indian exam calendar)
