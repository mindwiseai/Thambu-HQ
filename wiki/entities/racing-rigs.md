# Racing Rigs

Second Bangalore-based RaceSims dealer, complementing [[sim-racing-hq|Sim Racing HQ]].

**Owner:** [[lakshay]]

## Relationship with RaceSims
- **Dealer role:** resells RaceSims hardware in the Bangalore market
- **Customer role:** [[lakshay]] personally bought a motion system; install pending as of the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]]
- Install coordination via [[abdul]] (Bangalore-based technical installer)

## Two-dealer Bangalore strategy
RaceSims has **two** dealers in Bangalore ([[sim-racing-hq]] + Racing Rigs). This is uncommon unless the market is large enough to support both — Bangalore's sim-racing demand appears to be strong enough that two dealers don't cannibalize each other.
