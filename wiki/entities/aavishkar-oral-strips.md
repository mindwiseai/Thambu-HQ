# Aavishkar Oral Strips Pvt Ltd

Hyderabad-based (Telangana) pharmaceutical company that signed an NDA with [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen Marketing Company]] on **April 24, 2023** for the development and commercialization of **Oral Disintegrating Strips**.

## Key details

- **Registered address:** Plot No. 109/3, IDA, Phase - 2, Sector - 2, Lane - 6, Cherlapally, Hyderabad - 500 051, R.R. District, Telangana, India
- **Business purpose:** Evaluation of development and commercializing of **Oral Disintegrating Strips** (likely containing [[cdri-08]] Bacopa extract)
- **NDA effective date:** April 24, 2023 (same date as the [[chemiloids-life-sciences]] NDA)
- **NDA jurisdiction:** Courts at Hyderabad
- **NDA term:** 1 year from effective date, with 10-year confidentiality survival

## Significance for Lumen

Oral Disintegrating Strips (ODS) represent a **novel delivery format** for CDRI-08 Bacopa, distinct from capsules ([[domains/mindwise/_index|current product]]), gummies ([[solusta-puducherry|Solista]]), and syrup ([[product-range-expansion|planned]]). ODS dissolve on the tongue without water — attractive for on-the-go consumers and potentially for children who resist capsules or pills.

This NDA was signed the same day as the Chemiloids NDA, suggesting Lumen conducted a **batch NDA exercise** in April 2023 across multiple potential manufacturing partners for different product formats.
