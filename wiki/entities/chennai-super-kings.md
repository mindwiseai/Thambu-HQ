# Chennai Super Kings (CSK)

IPL cricket franchise based in Chennai. **Partnership target** for [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] via the [[2026-04-08-csk-pitch-draft|April 8 content idea pitch]] from [[thambu]].

## Why CSK specifically

- **Chennai-based** — same city as [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]]. Geographic proximity makes in-person meetings easy, simulator deployment logistics trivial.
- **IPL reach** — CSK has one of the largest social media followings among IPL franchises.
- **Fan engagement norms** — CSK is known for creative fan content, branded series, behind-the-scenes Reels. The content formats in the pitch fit their existing playbook.
- **Thambu's Chennai identity** — "local Chennai founder pitches CSK" is a **narrative hook** that CSK's PR team can lean into (local-boy-done-good angle).
- **No IPL team has done sim racing** — first-mover opportunity per the pitch email.
