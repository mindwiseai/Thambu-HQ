## Topic 2: [[website-development|Website Development]]

**Sorted's proposal:** ₹6,00,000 for custom Shopify store (UI/UX, dev, Razorpay, Klaviyo, analytics, reviews, full copy). Timeline: 10-15 days design + 30-35 days dev.

**Budget gap:** Thambu/Shivendra target ₹3L. Manik's position: ₹3L not feasible (developer alone is ~₹3L). ₹6L includes design + dev + full copy.

**Decision:** Pin until visual identity presentation (April 2). Easier to justify investment to LMC partners once they see the brand come to life.

**Manik to deliver:** one-pager / rough website copy mockup by April 1 (helps visualization).

**Designer note (from Sorted):** keep animations minimal for fast load and low bounce — performance/conversion website first.

## Topic 3: [[pricing-strategy|Pricing Strategy]]

**Manik's recommendation:** **Target MRP ₹850–900 per bottle.**

| Band | Verdict |
|---|---|
| Above ₹1,000 | Requires premium creator partnerships (₹2-3L per creator/month). Wellbeing Nutrition / Ace tier. Not viable. |
| Below ₹600 | Too mass-market. Doesn't match positioning. |
| **₹850–900** | **Sweet spot.** |

**Discounting:** Only on bundles, never on single-product price. Protect anchor.

**Key insight:** *"Premium pricing = premium content spend. Every pricing decision has downstream implications on the kind of creators, content quality, and marketing budget required."*
