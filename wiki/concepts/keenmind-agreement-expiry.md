# KeenMind Agreement Expiry

The **10-year agreement between [[sfi-health|SFI Health]] and [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]]** for the commercial distribution of [[cdri-08]] under the **KeenMind** brand **expired in December 2024**.

This is the foundational strategic event that enabled [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] to exist. From [[thambu]]'s research notes (Jan 14, 2026):

> "Agreement is over. We are coming into the market. Mindwise is our product. Sold in India."

## What this means

1. **[[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen Marketing Company]] is now the official licensed partner of [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]]** for CDRI-08 in India
2. The KeenMind brand and its global distributor network (see [[sfi-health]]) are now potentially up for grabs
3. [[pier-campanini]] reports that a new buyer may acquire KeenMind as part of a Ginsana factory sale (Feb 19, 2026)

## Competitive implications

With the SFI agreement expired:
- Existing KeenMind distributors (Europe, Middle East) may need new supply arrangements
- A new KeenMind buyer could become either a competitor or a partner
- Lumen/Mindwise has the opportunity to approach former SFI distributors with the [[cdri-08]] story
