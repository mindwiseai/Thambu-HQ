How did Mindwise begin? What's the brand story?
Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Science
MindWise's journey begins not in a corporate boardroom, but in the sacred traditions of Ayurveda, where for over 3,000 years, the Bacopa monnieri plant has been revered as a "medhya rasayana" – a memory enhancer and mental rejuvenator.
40 Years of Scientific Dedication
The modern chapter started in the 1960s when India's Central Drug Research Institute (CSIR-CDRI), a government research body, began systematically investigating this ancient herb. Through meticulous pharmacological, toxicology studies, phase 1 human trials for safety, phytochemical analysis, scientists identified the complex constituents that make Bacopa work.
What followed was an unprecedented 40-year commitment to research and development, resulting in CDRI 08 - a unique, standardized extract with a specific chemical fingerprint ensuring consistent quality and efficacy.
Global[a] Success Story
Through Lumen Marketing Company, this scientifically-validated ingredient traveled from India to international markets:
* MemoPlus Gold in the Philippines (20+ years of proven success)
* KeenMind in Australia, South Africa & Middle East
* Memory Perfect in Europe
* Actif Mind launching in Thailand and Malaysia
Realizing the potential for the elderly population in Australia, the Australian government funded a few clinical trials at Swinburne and Wollongong University. This validated the scientific study done by CDRI and got more global acceptance to the efficacy of the product.


Coming Home
Our strength has always been in formulation - we own the license for the Bacopa CDRI-08 extraction process from the Brahmi plant. While we've been manufacturing for global brands, we never owned those brands ourselves.
In March 2025, an agreement with KeenMind that restricted us from entering new markets ended, giving us the freedom to bring this product home to India under our own brand.
The MindWise Mission
We previously launched this formulation in India as "Memory Sure" over a decade ago, but the timing wasn't right - public awareness around supplements was still evolving.
Today, with students, professionals, and elderly actively seeking ways to support focus, memory, and mental clarity, we believe the timing is finally right to relaunch as MindWise.
Proudly Made in India
* Research by CSIR-CDRI, Lucknow.
* Raw Brahmi sourced from West Bengal.
* Extraction at state of the art facility in Vijayawada.
* Formulated in Pondicherry.
MindWise is entirely rooted in India—from early clinical work at CDRI Lucknow, to wild‑crafted Brahmi sourced from West Bengal, extraction in Vijayawada and manufacturing in Pondicherry. This is a product the team has lived with for decades, seen transform lives across age groups, and now wants to place directly in the hands of Indian students, professionals and elders at a time when brain health has become a mainstream need rather than a niche concern.
