## What the brief covers

- **Brand origin story:** [[csir-cdri|CDRI]] working on Brahmi since the 1960s; Times of India article in early 1990s; [[adam-ritson]] recovery story; Olga commercializing KeenMind in Australia
- **[[cdri-08]] differentiation:** 55% bacosides vs generic 20-30%
- **[[lumen-marketing-company]]'s 20-year license** for the CDRI-08 process patent
- **Global track record:** Philippines 20+ years (MemoPlus Gold), Australia (KeenMind)
- **SFI contract expiration** freeing India re-entry
- **Business objectives:** 10,000 bottles/month target, INR 1.2-1.3 crore annual ad budget
- **[[four-cohorts|Four target audiences]]:** mothers of school-going children, competitive exam takers, stressed/anxious individuals, elderly with cognitive decline
- **Platform strategy**
- **Product works from 1 week onwards**, peak performance at 3 months
- **Mechanism:** creates neurolinks, strengthens neurons
- **State at time of brief:** only 25 bottles sold on Amazon
