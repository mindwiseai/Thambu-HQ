# Wellbeing Nutrition

Indian wellness/supplement brand referenced in the first [[sorted-agency]] meeting (Dec 2, 2025) as an **aspirational brand example** — described as "seemed more trustworthy, packaging/style." Also referenced by [[manik]] in [[pricing-strategy]] discussions as a premium tier brand requiring creator partnerships at Rs.2-3L per creator/month.

## Why it matters for Mindwise

Wellbeing Nutrition represents the Indian market's benchmark for aspirational supplement branding. It sits in the premium tier where [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] aspires to position itself — trustworthy, well-packaged, premium aesthetic. However, Mindwise's [[your-unfair-advantage|differentiation]] is the [[cdri-08]] science base, which Wellbeing Nutrition lacks.
