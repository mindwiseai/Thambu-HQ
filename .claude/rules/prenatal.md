# Prenatal Brand Domain Rules

## Context
This is Aishu's (Aishwarya Chandrasekhar) prenatal & postnatal supplement brand for India. Modeled on Ritual (US). Pre-launch, research phase.

## Key constraints
- Product must register as FSSAI FSDU (Food for Special Dietary Use) — NOT Health Supplement
- All nutrients must stay within ICMR RDA (1x) — exceeding triggers CDSCO drug classification
- "Pregnancy safe" CANNOT appear on label — use "specially formulated for pregnant women"
- Gynecologist must be co-founder level, not advisor
- Lifestyle branding, not pharma-coded

## Regulatory red lines
- NO therapeutic claims in any content (wiki, marketing, strategy docs)
- NO dosage recommendations beyond ICMR RDA
- FSSAI Advertising and Claims Regulations (2018) apply to ALL content including social media
- When writing marketing content, verify claims against peer-reviewed literature
