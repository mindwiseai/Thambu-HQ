# DTC Lifestyle Brand Strategy

**Decision: lifestyle brand with clinical backing** — not pharma-coded. This is the core strategic bet for the [[domains/prenatal/_index|prenatal brand]].

## The decision

- Lead with DTC lifestyle brand as primary identity and revenue engine
- Use [[prenatal-gynecologist-cofounder|gynecologist co-founder]] for clinical credibility feeding into the lifestyle brand
- Can add doctor outreach and pharmacy distribution later as growth channel
- Starting pharma-coded makes lifestyle pivot very hard later; starting lifestyle allows adding pharma channels

## Why this matters

[[trimacare]] owns the pharma-coded, doctor-driven lane in India. Competing there head-on is expensive and plays to their strength. The [[ritual-benchmark|Ritual]] model proves DTC lifestyle can reach $250M+ in prenatal. No Indian brand occupies this position.

## DTC subscription model

- Direct website sales, not doctor/pharmacy distribution initially
- Subscription at Rs.800-1,500/month
- Target: urban metros (Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai)
- Expand to retail (pharmacies, wellness stores) only after establishing DTC base

## Brand pillars

1. **Clean, traceable ingredients** — every ingredient sourced from named suppliers
2. **Gynecologist co-formulated** — not just endorsed
3. **Lifestyle branding** — premium wellness brand, not pharma packaging
4. **Backed by science** — published literature at launch, own clinical study by Year 2-3
