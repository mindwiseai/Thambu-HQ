# Mindwise Launch Plan — June 1, 2026

The work-backwards launch plan for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]. Source: [[2026-03-24-launch-plan-june-1-2026]]. Planning date: 2026-03-24. **69 days to launch from planning date; ~50 days from today (2026-04-11).**

## Five parallel workstreams

1. **Brand Identity** — driven by [[sorted-agency]]
2. **Packaging** — driven by Sorted + [[patco-pharmaceuticals]]
3. **Website** — see [[website-development]]
4. **Marketing & Content** — see [[content-strategy-three-piece]], [[learning-is-childs-play-campaign]], [[lead-the-room-campaign]]
5. **Team & Operations** — see [[brand-manager-decision]], [[freelance-pr-strategy]]

## Phase 1: Foundation & Visual Identity (March 24 → April 2)

- **By March 28:** Sorted delivers first draft of visual identity / brand book; packaging renders (2 options); Manik vets performance/social media agency
- **By April 1:** Sorted delivers website copy one-pager mockup; updated packaging brief incorporating [[2026-03-23-binita-packaging-feedback|11-point feedback]]; internal alignment on website budget + pricing
- **April 2:** [[april-2-mumbai-meeting|IN-PERSON MEETING (MUMBAI)]] — visual identity presentation, website discussion, meet performance agency, packaging status

## Phase 2: Lock Decisions & Start Building (April 3 → April 15)

- **By April 5:** Consolidate feedback from April 2 → revision notes to Sorted. Website **DECISION** (Sorted ₹6L vs negotiate vs external developer).
- **By April 7:** PR freelancer engaged (see [[freelance-pr-strategy]])
- **By April 10:** Brand book / visual identity LOCKED. Packaging artwork sent to [[patco-pharmaceuticals|Patco]]. [[pricing-strategy|Pricing]] LOCKED at ₹850–900. Website copy first draft.
- **By April 12:** Blister pack design finalized
- **By April 15:** Website design approved → handoff to development. Social media handles refreshed with brand identity. Performance agency confirmed.

## Phase 3: Build & Create (April 16 → May 7)

- **April 15 → May 15:** Shopify development (30–35 days)
- **By April 20:** Brand film brief finalized
- **By April 25:** First development preview (homepage + product page); first packaging samples received
- **By April 30:** FAQ page content finalized
- **By May 1:** Razorpay + Klaviyo + analytics configured; influencer/creator outreach begins; production run starts for launch inventory
- **By May 7:** All dev pages complete; product photography with final packaging done; pre-launch teaser content goes live

## Phase 4: Pre-Launch (May 8 → May 25)

- **May 7-12:** Content loading into Shopify
- **May 12-15:** Cross-browser, mobile, performance testing
- **May 15:** Packaging production complete; inventory received and QC'd
- **May 18:** Website SOFT LOCK — only critical fixes after
- **May 18-25:** Performance ad creatives finalized
- **May 20:** Domain configured, SSL verified; Amazon listing updated with new branding
- **May 25:** Launch inventory packed and ready to ship

## Phase 5: Launch Week (May 26 → June 1)

- **May 26-28:** Final dress rehearsal
- **May 29:** Soft launch — website live, share with inner circle
- **May 30:** Performance ads go live (small daily budget, monitor)
- **May 31:** Final pre-launch social push
- **🚀 JUNE 1: LAUNCH DAY** — full social blast, PR coverage live, performance ads scaled, email blast, Amazon promo, WhatsApp Business ready

## Post-Launch (June 1 → June 30)

- **Week 1-2:** Daily monitoring, ad creative optimization
- **Week 2-3:** Second wave influencer content; address shipping issues
- **Week 3-4:** First monthly performance review; pricing/bundle adjustments; July content calendar; **school-season campaign planning** (June = school start in India)

## Key risks & mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Visual identity delayed past April 5 | Hard deadline with Sorted; escalate to Aarti |
| Website budget not approved by partners | Use visual identity + Manik one-pager; have external developer quotes as Plan B |
| Performance agency not a fit | Manik has other contacts; explore Pepper/Toptal/LinkedIn |
| Packaging production delays | Order placed by April 15 latest; 4-6 week lead time with Patco; buffer until May 15 |
| Scope creep on website | Lock scope at homepage + product page + 5 supporting; **no new features after April 15** |

## Critical path

The single critical path: **visual identity → website design → website development → content loading → launch**. The April 2 visual identity presentation is the upstream blocker. Anything that delays it cascades through everything. See [[critical-path-bottleneck]].
