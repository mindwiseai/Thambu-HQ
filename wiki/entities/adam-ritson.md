# Adam Ritson

Australian individual whose remarkable recovery from severe brain damage (sustained in a sports accident in the late 1990s / early 2000s) became the **catalyst that took CDRI-08 / [[cdri-08|Bacopa monnieri extract]] global**. Adam Ritson's story is the human core of the Mindwise origin narrative.

## The story (per [[2026-01-06-meeting-1-brief-discussion]] origin telling)

1. Late 1990s: [[csir-cdri|CDRI]] (Lucknow, India) is doing 40 years of standardized Bacopa monnieri research
2. Year 2000: CDRI completes ADHD clinical trials, gives commercialization go-ahead
3. **Olga Sortal** (Sydney, Australia) — Adam Ritson's aunt — learns about the extract
4. Adam suffers severe brain damage from a sports accident
5. Olga gives Adam high doses experimentally (NOT doctor-approved)
6. Adam shows **remarkable recovery**
7. Media sensation in Australia → Olga commercializes as "**Mimad**" brand
8. Peak sales: 100,000 packs/month in Australia
9. Product goes global as multiple sister brands:
   - **KeenMind** — Australia / South Africa / Middle East
   - **MemoPlus Gold** — Philippines (15L+ capsules sold; popular among students, ADHD, autism cases)
   - **Memory Perfect** — Europe
   - **Actif Mind** — Thailand / Malaysia

## Significance for Mindwise

- **Anchor of the human-truth narrative.** Mindwise's brand isn't a startup story; it's a 40-year scientific arc that produced a real recovery.
- Adam Ritson is the "we have receipts" of the [[your-unfair-advantage]] positioning
- Important caveat: this story should be told carefully and never as a medical claim. Adam's recovery was experimental, not doctor-supervised.
