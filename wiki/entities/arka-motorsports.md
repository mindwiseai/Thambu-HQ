# Arka Motorsports

**India's most decorated motorsport team.** Founded **1987** in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. **123+ championship titles** — "the only team in the country to have this prestigious record." [[thambu]] actively works as a race engineer here, and this is **the single most important external entity** in the [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] moat story.

Formerly known as **Red Rooster Performance**.

## Source of truth
Per the official ARKA Motorsports website (https://www.arkamotorsports.com), captured via Browser MCP on 2026-04-12. All facts below are direct-quote grade unless marked as inference.

## The headline claim

> **"With a little over 123 Championship Titles to its credit since 1987! Making us the only team in the country to have this prestigious record."**

This is extraordinary. Not "one of the top teams" — **THE team with this record**, by their own claim, and with a specific verifiable number. If accurate, Arka is the winningest active motorsport team in India.

## What they do

> "At Arka Motorsports we like to challenge limits. We believe in combining our motorsport pedigree with a free thinking approach, we create innovative engineering solutions to rapid timescales, helping our customers to realise their ambitions."

Specific activities per the site:
- **National Rally Championship** programs (primary involvement)
- **National Racing Championship** programs (primary involvement)
- **Engineering for major automobile manufacturers** to compete at the highest level of Indian motorsport
- **Customer motorsport** — supporting private teams and individuals in racing + rallying
- **Aftermarket automotive solutions** — customized products, bespoke builds
- **"Bespoke solutions to some of the top business houses in the country and Asiatic region"** — a B2B engineering consulting arm

## The team leader — [[mr-leela-krishnan|Mr. N. Leelakrishnan]]

The site describes Leelakrishnan directly:

> "With a young and ambitious team led by a multifaceted **7-time National rally champion and a multiple racing champion Mr. N. Leelakrishnan** requires little or no introduction in his contribution to Indian Motorsports which has spanned over **35 years!**"

Key facts:
- **7-time National Rally Champion**
- **Multiple** National Racing Champion (exact count not specified on the site)
- **35+ years** in Indian motorsports
- Founder / leader of Arka Motorsports
- See [[mr-leela-krishnan]] for the entity page

**Correction from the wiki bootstrap:** the March 26 voice brief transcription rendered his name as "Mr. Leela Krishnan" (two words). The **official spelling is "N. Leelakrishnan"** — one word, initial "N." The voice brief was a speech-to-text artifact.
