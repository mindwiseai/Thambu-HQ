## Summary

Locked or confirmed several pending strategic decisions: logo direction, two-track packaging strategy, capsule count, and pricing direction. Pricing was deferred to a later Sorted recommendation (which arrived in [[2026-03-24-meeting-manik-pricing-website|the March 24 Manik meeting]] as ₹850–900).

## Key decisions confirmed

### Logo / color direction
- ✅ **[[route-3-architecture-of-intelligence|Route 3 ("Architecture of Intelligence")]]** confirmed as locked
- ✅ Color: leaning toward neon green, open to additional creative options
- ✅ Sorted can proceed with bottle renders now that logo is finalized

### Bottle design — [[two-track-bottle-strategy|two-track approach]] LOCKED
- **Track 1 (Aspirational):** premium flush cap, multiple shapes (cylindrical / square / rectangular). Challenge: hard to source domestically without expensive custom molds. China sourcing as backup (1-week delivery).
- **Track 2 (Realistic backup):** standard PET/HDPE bottles from original presentation. Protects June 1 launch regardless. Can upgrade post-launch.
- ✅ AceBlend bottles confirmed sourced from Alibaba/China (found same bottle under different brand names)

### Capsule count — [[60-capsule-bottle-decision|60 caps]] LOCKED
- 60 capsules per bottle for D2C (NOT 30)
- 30-cap blister packs continue for distributor / B2B
- Premium positioning + 2-month habit-building requires longer usage period

### Bottle sizing
- 60 caps fit easily in 100mL bottle (Mindwise capsules very small vs. AceBlend ~150-200mL)
- Tested fit confirmed

### Pricing — DEFERRED
- Market context shared:
  - Low-end (Himalaya): ₹300-400
  - High-end premium: ₹4,000-5,000
  - Initial proposed target: ₹1,200 (middle)
- Discount strategy debated:
  - Heavy discounting damages brand imagery + price anchoring
  - Customers buying at deep discount rarely repurchase at full price
  - Recommendation: high MRP + minimal discounts (max 10%)
- ⏳ Action: Binita to discuss internally with [[manik]] + [[aarti-samant]], provide recommendation next week
- → Resolved on March 24 as [[pricing-strategy|₹850-900]]
