- **Strict SOP-driven process** — no guesswork, data-informed creative iteration
- Notable clients: SmartVeda (nutraceutical — scaled from 10K to 42K MRR in 6 months, 9.11x ROAS), Signature Muffin (menswear), Blue Mustra (sexual wellness), Contour (footwear), Vitabiotics/Wellman (UK vitamins)

## Three meetings held

### Meeting 1 — April 2, 2026 (Intro)
- Snigdha pitched the agency. Rohub briefly joined to introduce himself.
- Thambu explained Mindwise: CDRI-08, rebranding from pharmaceutical to supplement, June launch
- Snigdha's approach: persona-driven creative testing, 4-5 personas, theme x persona matrix, 48-hour performance windows

- Detailed process walkthrough for stakeholders to understand organic vs growth marketing

- Recommendation: start with **growth only** (paid), add organic later
- Content: 2-3 shoots/month producing 10+ core videos, 3 hook variations each = **30+ video assets/month**
- Website offer: ~Rs.1.7L, product page priority, 1-month build time
- SmartVeda case study: Chyawanprash + Shilajit scaled, best ROAS 9.11x
- COGS sheet to be filled by Thambu for P&L modeling

- **Sorted handover:** Dibiz to join Sorted's remaining meetings in April for warm handoff
