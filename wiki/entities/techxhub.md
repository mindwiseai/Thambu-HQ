## Strategic significance

Techxhub gives RaceSims access to **two globally recognized brands** (Fanatec, Playseat) without direct import complexity. Fanatec fills the mid-range direct-drive gap between [[moza]] (entry) and [[conspit]] (premium). Playseat cockpits offer a branded alternative to RaceSims' own GT rig frames for customers who want a known name.
