## Pricing structure

- Distributor margin: 25% on landed cost
- Dealer margin: 10-20%
- GST: 18%
- Landed cost examples: Ares Platinum 18Nm ~Rs.43K, GNX200 wheel ~Rs.44-48K, CPP-EVO 3 ~Rs.96K
- See [[import-operations]] for full duty breakdown and [[kit-pricing]] for bundle pricing.
