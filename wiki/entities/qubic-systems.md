# Qubic Systems

International sim-racing motion-platform supplier in the [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] import pipeline. **NDA stage only** as of April 2026 — no shipments have landed, no commercial relationship has launched yet.

## Product area

- **Motion platforms** — the missing category in RaceSims' current lineup
- Most current RaceSims imports are wheelbases, pedals, frames, and accessories; motion is the next frontier

## Relationship status

- **NDA signed, nothing beyond** (per [[import-operations]])
- No orders, no pricelist in the raw dump
- Country of origin: unknown

## Strategic significance

If RaceSims wants to offer motion rigs (vs. static rigs) at scale, it needs a reliable motion-platform supplier. Qubic is the current placeholder for that capability. Either Qubic converts into a real supply relationship, or RaceSims needs to qualify an alternative.
