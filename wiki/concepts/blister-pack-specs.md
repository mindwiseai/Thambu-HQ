# Blister Pack Specifications

The **B2B / distributor channel SKU** specifications for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]. Distinct from the [[60-capsule-bottle-decision|60-capsule D2C bottle]] — blister packs are the lower-entry-point format for pharmacy / distributor sales.

## Specifications captured

Two blister pack formats, dimensions shared via WhatsApp on March 19, 2026 (per [[2026-03-23-mindwise-meetings-chat-analysis|chat record Phase 10]]):

| Format | Dimensions (mm) |
|---|---|
| **60-capsule blister** | 70 × 10 × 120 |
| **120-capsule blister** | 115 × 75 × 125 |

## Why blister packs exist alongside bottles

The [[60-capsule-bottle-decision|60-cap bottle decision]] established that:
- **D2C channel:** 60-cap bottles (premium, 2-month adult supply, supports the [[8-12-week-onset-claim|8-12 week onset]])
- **B2B / pharmacy / distributor:** 30-cap blisters (lower entry price for pharmacy shelf trial)

Blister packs:
- Have a lower per-unit price (better for pharmacy retail entry)
- Travel well in distribution
- Are familiar to pharmacy buyers
- Don't directly compete with the D2C SKU
