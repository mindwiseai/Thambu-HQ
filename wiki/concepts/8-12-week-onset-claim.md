# 8–12 Week Onset to Peak Results

The realistic expectation-setting timeline for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] users. Critical to the [[brain-health-community-vision|community-first / retention strategy]] — and dramatically different from competitor "instant memory boost" claims.

## The claim

> "More focus, clearer thinking, better decision-making, increased productivity, reduced brain fog, greater confidence — **8–12 weeks to peak results**."

This is consistently used across all Mindwise communication frameworks.

## Why this timeline

The clinical evidence supports it directly:

| Trial | Duration | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| [[stough-2008-90-day-trial]] | 90 days | Significant working memory improvement at 90 days |
| [[stough-2001-12-week-trial]] | 12 weeks | Visual information processing, learning rate, memory consolidation, anxiety reduction at 12 weeks |
| [[roodenrys-2002-retention-trial]] | 6 weeks + 3 month follow-up | Improved retention at 6 weeks, decreasing forgetting at 3 months |
| [[barbhaiya-2008-elderly-trial]] | (elderly cohort) | Cognition improvements established |

The systematic review in Pase et al. 2011 concluded: *"Both these effects are evident at three months of daily administration, but not earlier."* This is the source of the **3-month onset window** that Mindwise inherits.

## Why this matters for retention (the killer business issue)

Combined with [[60-capsule-bottle-decision|60-cap bottles = 1 month adult supply]]:

- A buyer who buys ONE bottle and quits at 30 days has not given the science a chance
- Most buyers will conclude "it didn't work" if they don't see results in 30 days
- This is why the [[brain-health-community-vision|community-first / education-first]] approach is essential
- This is also why [[pricing-strategy|bundle pricing]] (buy 2 / buy 3) is the right structural answer — it commits buyers to the science timeline

## How to communicate it

✅ "Set realistic expectations: 8–12 weeks to peak results. This is brain biology, not a stimulant."
✅ Day-by-day what-to-expect content for the [[content-strategy-three-piece|sustenance content]] layer
✅ Onboarding email sequence: Week 1, Week 4, Week 8, Week 12 check-ins
✅ Doctor-facing material — adheres to clinical reality, builds prescriber trust
❌ Never imply faster onset to chase impulse buyers — this destroys the retention base

## What it explicitly differentiates from

The [[placebo-wellness-enemy|"Placebo Wellness"]] tier sells:
- "Instant focus boost"
- "Feel sharper in 30 minutes"
- "Same-day clarity"

These claims are either:
- Clinically false for Bacopa (it doesn't work that way)
- True but for caffeine / stimulants disguised as nootropics

Mindwise's 8–12 week claim is **anti-marketing** in the short-term — it deliberately filters out impulse-buy traffic that wouldn't retain anyway.
