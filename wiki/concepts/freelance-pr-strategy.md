# Freelance PR Strategy

[[manik]] proposed engaging a **freelance PR contact** for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] launch instead of a full agency. Surfaced in [[2026-03-24-meeting-manik-pricing-website|the March 24 meeting]].

## The cost comparison

| Option | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Freelance PR contact** (Manik's recommendation) | **~₹1.7 lakhs** | Media placements, podcast appearances, earned media |
| **Mad Over Marketing** (full agency) | **~₹12 lakhs** | Reference for what an agency-route would cost |

That's a **~7× difference** for what Manik judges to be comparable practical output at the launch stage.

## What the freelancer covers
- Media placements (press)
- Podcast appearances (especially India brain/longevity/wellness creators)
- Earned media outreach
- Founder narrative pitching
