# Shahid Moto Mind

Bangalore-based external design vendor that was commissioned to design the **exterior cladding** for the [[next-gen-racesims-rigs|next-generation RaceSims rigs]]. As of the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]], **Thambu is rejecting their design direction**.

> [!note]
> The exact spelling is uncertain from the voice brief transcription. Possible variants: "Shahid Motomind", "Shahid Motom Mind", "Shahid Moto Mind". Treating as "Shahid Moto Mind" pending confirmation.

## Status: ⚠️ direction rejected

Per the voice brief:
> "In Bangalore, I have to Shahid Moto mind and finalize the exterior cladding design for the rig. **I'm not going ahead with the design direction that they have told.** We have some renders and mockups; those are there in the projects folder; you can take a look. We want to go in that direction."

In plain English: Shahid Moto Mind produced a design direction that Thambu disagreed with. Thambu has prepared alternative renders/mockups internally and is going to override the vendor's direction during the in-person Bangalore visit.

## Implication for next-gen rigs

[[next-gen-racesims-rigs|The next-gen rig design]] is being shaped by:
1. **[[mr-leela-krishnan|Leela Krishnan]]** (ARKA boss) — functional design inputs (cast aluminum brackets, mounting hardware)
2. **Thambu's own renders/mockups** — aesthetic / cladding direction
3. **Shahid Moto Mind** — execution vendor (but NOT direction-setter)

This is a **real-world design governance pattern** — Thambu is keeping design authority in-house and using Shahid as an execution shop, not as a creative director.
