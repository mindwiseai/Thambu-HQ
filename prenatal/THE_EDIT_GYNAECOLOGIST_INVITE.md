## 01. Why I started this

When I was planning my pregnancy, I had a quiet, frustrating problem.

**I couldn't find a prenatal supplement in India that I trusted.**

The options felt clinical, opaque, or imported. I ended up relying on supplements brought from abroad — because the brands available to me at home felt built more for compliance than for care.

After my pregnancy, I realized I wasn't alone. Most modern Indian women I spoke to had the same struggle.

So I started The Edit.

---

## 02. Why my husband is part of this

My husband Thambu has spent over a decade in the Indian supplement industry.

His company, Lumen Marketing Company, holds two CSIR-CDRI licenses — for *Bacopa monnieri* and *Terminalia chebula*. That's the kind of regulatory depth most Indian supplement startups never get close to.

His brand Mindwise (cognitive wellness, launching June 2026) has taken him through every stage of building a supplement business in India — regulatory, manufacturing, formulation, packaging, distribution.

*He brings the operational depth. I bring the founder's vision.*

**The Edit's third co-founder needs to bring the clinical credibility — and that's where you come in.**

---

## 03. What we're building

The Edit is a modern wellness brand for Indian couples and parents-to-be — across every stage of the family-building journey.

**Three principles:**

- **Transparency** — every ingredient traceable to a named source
- **Science** — every dose backed by published evidence
- **Care** — every formula designed for a specific stage

**Three products at launch:**

- **Edit For Two** — preconception, for both partners
- **Edit Prenatal** — comprehensive pregnancy support
- **Edit Postnatal** — postpartum + lactation (Year 1 expansion)

*Built in India, for Indian women.*

---

## 04. Why we want a co-founder, not an advisor

We could pay a doctor to endorse our brand. That's how most Indian supplement brands operate.

**We don't want to do that.**

We want a practicing OB-GYN at the founding table — someone whose clinical voice shapes our formulations from Day 1, whose name appears as a co-founder, whose equity reflects the value they bring.

**The brand we're building deserves to be co-built by someone who actually treats Indian women.**

---

## 05. The role

### What we offer

- Co-founder title and founder-level equity in The Edit Pvt Ltd

- Willingness to co-design formulations, not just review them
