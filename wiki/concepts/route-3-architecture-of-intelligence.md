# Route 3 — "Architecture of Intelligence"

The locked logo direction for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]. Selected from three options presented by [[sorted-agency]] in late January 2026 and confirmed in [[2026-03-17-meeting-binita-strategic-decisions|the March 17 meeting]] with [[binita]].

## What it represents

- **Visual metaphor:** brain expansion, neural architecture, neural connectivity
- **Color preference:** neon green (similar to Sorted's own logo and OneB logo) — open to additional creative options
- **Aesthetic:** premium, performance-driven, NOT clinical/medicinal

- Represents the [[your-unfair-advantage|"unfair advantage"]] concept visually
- Brain expansion / neural performance as central visual metaphor

## Logo design principles (per Visual Identity Meeting)

The logo MUST:
- Be assertive, not quiet confidence — bold visual presence
- Be optimization-oriented, not harmony-oriented
- Be pioneering, iconic — groundbreaking visual approach
- Have active body language — dynamic, expansive positioning
- Take space — immediate visual thumb-stopping power on internet

The logo MUST NOT:
- Look medicinal or templatized
- Use pharmaceutical visual language
- Be quiet / archaic / templatized
- Get lost in a crowd
- Feel like a logo-maker output
