### Summary

Sales proposal for Mr. Ajith, a professional racing driver competing in the Porsche Sprint Challenge. Pitches a sim training setup replicating the Porsche 992 GT3 Cup car.

### Recommended Hardware

- **Wheelbase & Pedals:** Asetek Invicta
- **Steering Wheel:** VPG V-PGT (exact Porsche Cup replica)
- **Display:** 32" triple screen sim cockpit (1:1 driving experience)
- **Software:** iRacing (laser-scanned tracks, Porsche-validated 992 GT3 Cup model)

### Value Proposition (four pillars)

1. **Track training** -- track familiarization, camber adaptation, kerb usage, wet line training, sector consistency
2. **Car training** -- braking/downshift rhythm, brake bias optimization, fuel load adaptation, aero behavior, rotation management
3. **Driver training** -- muscle memory development, racecraft & overtaking, strategy execution
4. **iRacing integration** -- MoTeC telemetry comparison, race starts, tire management simulation

### Positioning

RaceSims positioned as a professional motorsport training partner (race engineer founder, 14+ years experience), not just a hardware seller. Proposal emphasizes Thambu's background as a race engineer and tuner.
