## Boranutri — Tanishq's existing prenatal SKU (verified 2026-04-14)

URL: https://tanishqlifecare.com/boranutri.php

- **Positioning:** Pregnancy & Lactation support
- **Pack:** 3 x 10 tablets
- **Formula (22 active ingredients):** Folic acid, Iron, B-complex (B1/B2/B3/B6/B12), D3, E, K, C, Biotin, Pantothenic acid, Magnesium, Zinc, Copper, Selenium, Iodine, Inositol, N-Acetyl Cysteine, CoQ10, Beta-carotene, natural carotenoids
- **Marketing claims:** "Reduces preterm birth and low birth weight" + references UNICEF/WHO recommendations
- **Notable formulation gap:** NO Omega-3 / DHA — modern prenatals (e.g., [[ritual-benchmark|Ritual]]) include algal DHA for fetal brain development

> [!important]
> Boranutri's claim "reduces preterm birth and low birth weight" is a **therapeutic claim** prohibited under FSSAI [[prenatal-fsdu-regulatory|FSDU rules]] and FSSAI Ad Regs 2018. This means Boranutri is likely either: (a) registered as a drug under DCGI/CDSCO (matches Tanishq's "DCGI Approved" cert), (b) export-only to markets with looser claim rules, or (c) non-compliant with FSSAI in India. Must confirm which before assuming Tanishq's regulatory experience translates to FSDU.
