# Brain Health Community Vision

The long-term strategic vision for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] as articulated in [[2026-01-06-23-meeting-2-written-brief|Meeting 2's written brief]]:

> **"Become India's Most Trusted Brain Health Community"**

This is what Mindwise wants to be in 5 years, not just at launch. It frames Mindwise as a **platform**, not a SKU.

## What this looks like in practice

- Clear, non-sensational information on memory, focus, cognitive aging
- Ongoing formats: live sessions with clinicians, study cohorts, workplace performance series, aging-well circles
- Eventual evolution into a recognizable "mind health platform" (courses, events, research collaborations)
- Special focus on children and elderly with dementia prevention

## Three-pillar marketing strategy (the operational layer)

From Meeting 2's written brief:

1. **Educate** — Simplify the science (CDRI-08, trials, timelines) for layperson understanding
2. **Acquire** — Generate qualified high-intent visitors → first-time buyers at efficient CAC
3. **Retain & Build Trust** — Set correct expectations ([[8-12-week-onset-claim|8-12 week horizon]]), support adherence, turn users/doctors into advocates

This is the framework most marketing decisions should map to — every piece of content / spend / partnership should serve at least one pillar.

## Community-first, transactions-second

A recurring theme across [[2026-01-to-02-meetings-1-to-5|all five strategy meetings]]:

> **"Build community first, transactions second; educate more than sell."**

The logic:
- A repeat user is worth ~5× a one-time buyer
- Cognitive supplements have a long onset ([[8-12-week-onset-claim|8-12 weeks]]) — buyers who don't believe the science quit early
- Trust + education = adherence = retention
- Doctor prescriber base = stable demand that doesn't depend on ad spend

## Healthcare professional foundation

Year-1 objective from [[2026-01-06-meeting-1-brief-discussion|Meeting 1]]:

> Build neurologist / psychiatrist / physician / psychologist prescriber base.

Doctor endorsers ([[dr-dilip-ghosh|Dilip Ghosh]], [[dr-hemant-singh|Hemant Singh]]) are part of this foundation. The longer-term play is becoming the brand neurologists actually recommend for cognitive support.

## Community formats (not yet operationalized — long-term)

- Live sessions with clinicians
- Study cohorts (people taking Mindwise together, sharing progress)
- Workplace performance series (B2B)
- Aging-well circles (40+ cohort)
- Special focus on children + elderly dementia prevention

## What this means for content strategy

The [[content-strategy-three-piece|three-piece content format]] is the SHORT-term tactic. The community vision is the LONG-term frame. They reinforce each other:

- Sustenance content → community formation
- Brand films → values/identity for the community to identify with
- Key visuals → acquisition funnel that feeds the community

## The risk this guards against

**Becoming a one-time-purchase nutraceutical.** Most Indian supplements live in a "buy once, never re-buy" market. The community vision is the antidote — it's how Mindwise builds defensible LTV instead of competing in CAC arbitrage.
