# Discord Community Gap

**The single biggest community deficit RaceSims has vs [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]].** Discord is where Indian sim racers actually live. Not having a server makes RaceSims invisible to the enthusiast community that drives word-of-mouth referrals.

## The current state

| | RaceSims | VRH |
|---|---|---|
| Discord server | ❌ NONE | ✅ 537 members |
| Weekly community races | ❌ | ✅ Sundays |
| Race engineer technical channel | (would be unique) | ❌ |

VRH already runs weekly community races every Sunday. Discord is **the** hangout for sim racers — it's where setup discussions happen, where buying decisions are validated, and where new buyers ask questions.

## What's already done (the unblock)

✅ The [[discord-server-setup-kit|Discord Server Setup Kit]] **already exists** in the repo (`racesims/kits/Discord_Server_Setup_Kit.html`). It's a fully-drafted launch playbook with channel structure, moderation rules, weekly event templates, and a content calendar.

**The kit has been ready for weeks. It just hasn't been deployed.**

This is the single highest-leverage unblock in the entire RaceSims [[90-day-execution-plan|90-day plan]]. ~30 minutes of execution unlocks weeks of community-building.

## The asymmetric advantage

When RaceSims launches Discord, it should immediately differentiate from VRH on **content** (not just exist):

**The killer channel: `#race-engineer-tips`**
- Weekly posts from [[thambu]] with real racing knowledge
- Setup guides, telemetry analysis, driving tips
- Things VRH literally cannot reproduce (see [[race-engineer-positioning]])

**Other recommended channels** (per the kit + battle plan):
- `#welcome`
- `#general-chat`
- `#setup-help`
- `#race-engineer-tips` ← the moat
- `#deals-and-offers`
- `#customer-setups`
- `#race-events`
- `#buy-sell-trade`

**Plus:**
- Weekly community races (matching VRH but with **post-race race-engineer commentary** as the differentiator)
- Discord-only discount codes
- Product launch previews (Discord first, then website — creates FOMO + exclusivity)

## Companion strategy: partner with [[indian-racing-community|IRC]]

Don't only build from scratch. The Indian Racing Community (IRC) is the **largest existing Indian sim-racing Discord** (per the VRH battle plan). Sponsor their events, become their official hardware partner, get in front of hundreds of active sim racers immediately.

This is the **build + partner** double approach:
- Build own Discord = controlled premium channel
- Partner with IRC = top-of-funnel reach
