# The Edit Launch Architecture — Scenario D (LOCKED 2026-04-14)

[[aishwarya-chandrasekaran|Aishu]] selected this launch architecture on 2026-04-14, driven by the [[prenatal-brand-manifesto-couples-equality|couples-equality manifesto]]. Replaces the earlier [[prenatal-launch-roadmap|simple prenatal-only roadmap]].

## Day 1 launch SKUs

1. **Edit Preconception Couples Bundle** (Him + Her, sold and marketed as one product)
   - Edit For Her Preconception (FSDU regulatory pathway)
   - Edit For Him Preconception (Health Supplement regulatory pathway)
2. **Edit Prenatal** (FSDU regulatory pathway)

Three SKUs total at Day 1. Couples bundle is the **hero**.

## Scenarios considered and rejected

| Scenario | Description | Why rejected |
|---|---|---|
| C | Prenatal only at Day 1 | Misses couples-equality positioning entirely |
| F | Preconception (Her) + Prenatal at Day 1, add Him later | Delaying Him contradicts the manifesto |
| Preconception-only | Skip prenatal entirely | Customer churns at conception (success = leakage), TAM too small, success rate doesn't scale |
| All 3 (E) | Including Postnatal at Day 1 | Postnatal has no Day 1 demand — wastes capital on inventory waiting for cohort to give birth |

## Year 1 expansion roadmap

| Month | Add | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Edit Couples Bundle + Edit Prenatal | Day 1 launch, couples-equality positioning live |
| 9-12 | Edit Postnatal | First Prenatal cohort gives birth, naturally graduates to Postnatal |
| 12-18 | Possible Edit Fertility Add-Ons | If brand is hungry for more SKUs (lubricants etc — note: lubricant = CDSCO Medical Device, separate regulatory) |

## Resource implications

- **Build timeline:** 16-18 months from start to launch
- **Initial capital (Tier 2 realistic launch, 12-month runway):** Rs.70-90L. Breakdown: build + setup Rs.25-38L (entity, FSSAI, inventory, brand identity, packaging, photography, tech stack, logistics, insurance, legal) + marketing Rs.25-40L (performance ads, influencer collaborations, PR, content) + founder/team runway Rs.13-20L. Earlier estimates of Rs.20-25L were under-budgeted (missed realistic marketing costs and full team runway). Updated 2026-04-19.
- **Regulatory pathways:** TWO — FSDU (Prenatal + Preconception Her) + Health Supplement (Preconception Him)
- **Manufacturer:** must handle both FSDU + Health Supplement categories. Adds dimension to [[tanishq-lifecare]] vs [[brukem-life-care]] evaluation.

## Why this is the right architecture for The Edit

- **Captures full female customer journey** (preconception → pregnancy) — no churn at conception
- **Highest LTV at launch** (~15 months per customer) without launching Postnatal prematurely
- **Couples-equality lives from Day 1** as committed to in the manifesto
- **Builds platform brand from launch** while still focused enough for a small team
- **Two PR moments built in:** Day 1 launch + Month 9-12 Postnatal launch

## Implications for manufacturer discovery

The 2026-04-14 manufacturer emails to [[tanishq-lifecare|Tanishq]] and [[brukem-life-care|Brukem]] need to be updated to reflect 3 SKUs, not 1. They need to confirm:
- FSSAI Central License covers FSDU AND Health Supplement categories
- Can formulate male fertility ingredients (CoQ10, L-carnitine, ashwagandha, zinc, selenium)
- Can quote on couples bundle packaging (matched-pair design)
- MOQs and per-unit cost across all 3 SKUs
