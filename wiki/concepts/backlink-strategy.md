# Backlink Strategy

The plan to build domain authority for racesims.in. Sourced from [[2026-03-25-vrh-battle-plan|VRH Battle Plan]].

## Why this matters

**Backlinks remain the most important Google ranking signal.** Every authoritative site that links to racesims.in passes some of its authority. [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]] currently has more high-authority backlinks (YourStory, Growwh) which is part of why they outrank on commercial keywords — see [[seo-content-gap]].

## High-priority targets

### Press / startup media

| Target | Pitch angle | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **[[yourstory|YourStory]]** | "Race engineer turns sim racing entrepreneur" | VRH precedent, accepts contributor pitches |
| **Growwh** | Same | VRH has been featured |
| **Inc42** | Indian motorsport + tech story | Indian startup media loves these |
| **TechSparks** | Same | Same |

### Industry sites

| Target | Action | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **SimRacingBlog.in** | Guest post (race-engineer expertise) | Already ranks for "sim racing in India" |
| **[[simgrid|SimGrid]] (thesimgrid.com)** | Create RaceSims team page | VRH has one, RaceSims doesn't — quick win |

### Communities / partnerships

| Target | Action | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **[[indian-racing-community|Indian Racing Community (IRC)]]** | Sponsor events, become official hardware partner | Largest Indian sim-racing community, fastest path to audience |

## Pitch templates (the race-engineer story arc)

Every press pitch leans on the same founder narrative:

> "[Thambu] is a race engineer from championship-winning racing teams who transitioned into building India's premium sim racing hardware company. He's positioning racesims.in to compete with global brands by leveraging professional motorsport credibility — telemetry-driven setups, sim-to-real training methodology, and partnerships with top international hardware brands like Conspit and VNM."

This is the core narrative for:
- YourStory contributor pitch
- Inc42 outreach
- TechSparks
- Podcast invitations
- Speaker proposals

## Timing (per [[90-day-execution-plan]])

- **Week 1-2:** Get listed on SimGrid (30 min, instant backlink)
- **Week 3-4:** Pitch YourStory for founder feature
- **Week 5-8:** Inc42, Growwh, TechSparks pitches
- **Week 5-8:** Sponsor IRC events / become hardware partner
- **Week 9-12:** Press coverage live

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Buy backlinks from low-quality SEO sites — Google will penalize
- ❌ Submit to generic web directories from 2010
- ❌ Reciprocal link schemes
- ❌ Low-effort guest post spam (e.g., generic content marketing on irrelevant sites)
- ❌ Press release distribution services (low quality)
