## Why these specific 4 questions (the strategy)

| Question | What it actually tests |
|---|---|
| FSSAI FSDU endorsement | The hard regulatory gate. If they can't manufacture under FSDU, nothing else matters. |
| Branded ingredients (Quatrefolic, life'sDHA, Albion) | Tests whether they can source premium traceable ingredients vs. cheap generics. The brand story depends on this. |
| Certificate copies with numbers + expiry | Filters bullshit. Real certifications come with documents; vapor certifications don't. |
| References from DTC women's health brands | The values test — how do they treat brand-side customers, not pharma B2B? |

## What to look for in the response

- **Speed:** under 5 business days = good signal
- **Completeness:** answers all 4 questions vs. dodges = good signal
- **Documents attached vs. promised:** attached = good signal
- **Tone:** treats you like a serious buyer vs. boilerplate brochure = good signal
- **Willingness to share references:** willing = good signal; "we don't share" = walk away
