# Mr. N. Leelakrishnan

**7-time National Rally Champion. Multiple National Racing Champion. 35+ years in Indian motorsports.** Founder and leader of [[arka-motorsports|Arka Motorsports]] — India's most decorated motorsport team (123+ championship titles since 1987).

## Name correction

> [!note]

Leelakrishnan"** — one word, with the initial "N." The filename `mr-leela-krishnan.md` is preserved here to avoid breaking backlinks, but the canonical spelling is **N. Leelakrishnan**.
