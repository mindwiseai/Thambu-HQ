# "Your Unfair Advantage"

The locked tagline / brand manifesto for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]. Decided in the [[2026-01-23-meeting-3-brand-strategy-workshop|January 23 brand strategy workshop]] led by [[tanya-leeds]] and [[aarti-samant]].

## Why this tagline works (per the workshop)

It does three things in three words:

1. **Not magical** — tells people this isn't a gimmick
2. **Work done** — implies 40+ years of [[csir-cdri|CDRI]] research already invested
3. **Level up** — unlocks potential and performance

The emotional read is: *"I have leverage in the world by taking this."*

## What it replaces

The previous brand language was clinical / technical / scientific:
- "The only clinically proven Bacopa-based supplement..."
- "Made with original CDRI-08 extract..."
- "Backed by 7 clinical trials..."

That language created the [[scary-drug-problem]]: people felt they needed a doctor's permission. The shift to "Your Unfair Advantage" was the **central pivot** of the entire brand strategy. See [[positioning-shift]] for the before/after detail.

## Cross-cohort fit

The tagline must work for [[four-cohorts|all four target audiences]]:

| Cohort | What "unfair advantage" means to them |
|---|---|
| Students / exam-goers | Edge in competitive exams without stimulants |
| High-pressure professionals | Brain fog clearance, leadership confidence |
| Proactive agers / caregivers | Independence, dementia prevention |
| ADHD individuals | Focus and cognitive stability |

This is also what makes it solve the [[age-paradox|18-to-85 age paradox]] — the language isn't tied to a life stage.

## Meeting 05 context (from [[2026-04-13-mindwise-raw-dump]])

The tagline was locked in **Meeting 05 (Jan 23, 2026)** — the Brand Communication Strategy Workshop. Per the Meetings Catalog, this was the most substantive strategy meeting (~2 hours). [[aarti-samant]] presented the full brand framework:
- **What:** "The Only Govt-Backed Daily Brain Supplement for a Sharper Mind"
- **How:** "By strengthening neurons and building new neural links" (CDRI-08, 55% potency, 40 years research)
- **Why:** "To help every brain perform at its full potential"

The competitive mapping in this meeting placed [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] between Himalaya (INR 180-400) at the low end and Mind Lab Pro (INR 8,300-15,500) at the premium imported end.
