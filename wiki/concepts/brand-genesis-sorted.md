# Brand Genesis — How Sorted Got Engaged

The first documented meeting between [[thambu]] and [[sorted-agency]] was **December 2, 2025**, with [[manik|Manik Sajnani]] and [[jaiman|Jaiman Khatri]] attending.

## The Dec 2, 2025 meeting

Key discussion points from the origin meeting:

- **Community of Marketers in India** — budget-based execution, "very frugal"
- [[manik]] and [[aarti-samant|Aarti]] met at **Future Group / Blackstone**. Aarti was building [[sorted-agency|Sorted]].
- Sorted's services: **strategy & content**. Consulting had stopped. They also create.
- "Brands want a younger audience" — the generational targeting insight
- [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] brand framing: "Outlook, 1 product — different forms"

## The positioning seeds

Key quotes from the Dec 2 meeting that planted the seeds for the entire [[positioning-shift]]:

- "Opinion in this space — conscious movement around levelling up"
- "Not your gut, longevity, but also brain health — education segment"
- "A segment in longevity = brain health (procedure space)"
- Brain Science program, NLP — "re-wire your thinking. Accelerate your growth"
- "I don't mind trying a supplement — why not. Help with brain rot"
- **"Positioning this as a brand — what strategy will do"**

## Reference brands

- [[wellbeing-nutrition]] — referenced as an aspirational example; "seemed more trustworthy, packaging/style"
- Brain Science program, NLP — non-supplement reference for the performance/optimization frame

## Deliverables discussed

- Sorted to deliver frameworks (to be updated)
- Strategy and content as primary services
- The engagement would start with **strategy first, then content, then branding** — a sequencing that [[sarathkumar-srinath]] independently confirmed in a Dec 3 meeting

## Significance

This meeting marks the beginning of the [[sorted-agency]] engagement that would ultimately produce:
- [[your-unfair-advantage]] tagline
- [[positioning-shift]] from clinical to performance
- [[semi-transparent-bottle]] design direction
- [[route-3-architecture-of-intelligence]] logo
- [[learning-is-childs-play-campaign]] and [[lead-the-room-campaign]]
