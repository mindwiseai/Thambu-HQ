# RaceSims 90-Day Execution Plan

The week-by-week execution plan for RaceSims to close the [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]] gap. Sourced from the [[2026-03-25-vrh-battle-plan|VRH Battle Plan]] (March 25, 2026 — currently ~2.5 weeks into execution).

## Week 1-2 — Foundation (Do This Now)

- ✅ / ⏳ Launch Instagram content calendar — first 10 Reels (product showcases + race engineer tips)
- ⏳ **Create Discord server** with full channel structure (see [[discord-server-setup-kit]] — already drafted)
- ⏳ Bulk-fix product image alt texts on Shopify (Hextom app)
- ⏳ Create r/SimRacingIndia subreddit (if available)
- ⏳ Write first blog article: **"Best Sim Racing Setup in India 2026"**
- ⏳ Get listed on [[simgrid]] (30-min quick win)

## Week 3-4 — Content Machine

- Publish 3 more blog articles targeting key Indian sim racing keywords
- Run first Instagram giveaway (follow + tag to win)
- Start weekly Discord community races
- Pitch [[yourstory]] for founder feature
- Post first Reddit AMA: "Race engineer running a sim racing store in India — AMA"

## Week 5-8 — Growth Phase

- Reach 1,500+ Instagram followers through consistent Reels + giveaways
- Partner with [[indian-racing-community|IRC]] for event sponsorship
- Publish comparison articles: [[conspit]] vs [[vnm]], pedal type comparisons
- Create dedicated FAQ page for AI search optimization (see [[geo-content-readiness]])
- Start Instagram ad campaign (₹500-1000/day)
- Contact 5 Indian motorsport micro-influencers for product collaborations (including [[jamie-shaw-racing]])

## Week 9-12 — Overtake

- Target 3,000+ Instagram followers
- Target 200+ Discord members with active weekly events
- 10 [[blog-content-pipeline|blog articles]] published, ranking for key terms
- Press coverage live (YourStory or equivalent)
- Reddit presence established with karma and recognition
- Review and optimize: double down on what's working, cut what isn't

## Quick wins (next 48 hours from VRH battle plan)

These are the fast moves that can happen TODAY:

1. **Post 3 Instagram Reels** — film yourself talking about a product, show your workspace, or react to a sim racing clip. Volume beats perfection.
2. **Create a Discord server** — 30 minutes. Use the channel structure from the [[discord-server-setup-kit|setup kit]].
3. **Add Instagram + Discord links to racesims.in header/footer** — people can't follow what they can't find.
4. **Claim Google Business Profile** — if not already.
5. **Add a comparison page to your site** — "Why RaceSims?" comparing to alternatives. Don't trash VRH; highlight race engineer expertise + professional focus.

## Critical first move (the unblock)

Of all the items above, **the single highest-leverage unblock is creating the Discord server**. The kit is ready, the bot is coded, the content angle is defined. ~30 minutes of execution unlocks weeks of community-building. See [[discord-community-gap]] and [[discord-server-setup-kit]].
