## Part 1: Sorted Agency Meetings (HIGH VALUE)

These are meetings between the Mindwise/Lumen team (Thambu, Shivendra, Ankita) and Sorted Agency (Aarti Samant, Manik, Tanya, Binita, Avantika). They trace the complete brand strategy evolution from first consultation to visual identity lock.

### Meeting 01 — Aarti First Call
- **Date:** ~Late 2025 (no precise date in transcript)
- **Participants:** Thambu, Aarti Samant
- **Files:**
  - `1-1 with Aarti.docx` — Full transcript (~65 min)
  - `1-1 with Aarti.mp4` — Video recording (977 MB)
- **Has transcript:** YES
- **Summary:** Foundational discovery call. Thambu explains the entire Mindwise/CDRI-08 story to Aarti: the CDRI patent, Lumen Marketing Company's 20-year license, the global products (KeenMind in Australia, MemoPlus Gold in Philippines), the SFI contract expiration freeing India re-entry, and the Adam Ritson story. Aarti gives blunt feedback: the brand looks too clinical/medical. She recommends a brand communication strategy before any execution, emphasizing the need for What/How/Why statements, a brand personality shift away from "clinical" to "human," and positioning more like Whoop or SuperYou than a pharma brand.
- **Key decisions:** Engage Sorted Agency for brand communication strategy. Drop CDRI from the brand name/handle. Define four target audiences: (1) mothers of school-going children, (2) competitive exam takers, (3) people under stress/anxiety, (4) elderly concerned about cognitive decline.
