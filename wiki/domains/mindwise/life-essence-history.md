# Life Essence — Previous Brand Name

"Life Essence" was the brand name used by [[lumen-marketing-company]] before the rebrand to **Mindwise**. Historical files live in `raw/mindwise/Life Essence/`.

## Timeline

- **Nov 2023:** Life Essence trademark filed (`LIFE ESSENCE trademark.pdf`)
- **Jan 2024:** Life Essence product document created
- **Jul 2023:** Early "MIND WISE" mockup JPGs created (earliest Mindwise reference found)
- **Jun 2024:** Mindwise PPT created — rebrand appears complete by this point
- **Late 2025:** [[sorted-agency]] engagement begins with "Mindwise" as the established brand name

## Files in the Life Essence archive

- **LIFE ESSENCE trademark.pdf** — trademark registration (Nov 2023)
- **life essence.pdf** — product document (Jan 2024)
- **Mindwise ppt.pptx** — early Mindwise presentation (Jun 2024)
- **Amazon/** — barcode subscription letter, product images
- **LIFE ESSENCE LOGO/** — 3 logo option PDFs + draft concepts
- **Life Essence/** (nested) — duplicate logos + MIND WISE subfolder with 6 early mockup JPGs
- **Website/** — single Razorpay CSV export (`rzp-2.csv`)

## Connection to LLS/LMC logos

The `Old creatives and designs/Logo/` folder contains "LLS" (Life Essence/Lumen) final logos from 2020-2022, and a "Lifeessence standboard PDF." These predate even the Life Essence trademark filing.
