# Sarathkumar Srinath

External advisor who participated in a December 3, 2025 meeting with [[manu-bennur]] and [[thambu]], discussing early [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] positioning and go-to-market strategy.

## Key contributions (Dec 3, 2025)

- Explored the competitive landscape: Brahmi Amla, Patanjali brain products — "competing against big brands using ayurveda"
- Smart positioning discussion: core audience, generational examples
- Recommended the sequencing: **strategy first, then branding, then marketing**
- Multi-vitamin reference (Centrum) — differentiation by gender (posters/standees for men and women)
- Proposed a **1-day discovery workshop**
- Posed the critical framing question: **"Is it a: Brain booster / Lifelong brain health / Clinical brain drug"**

## Strategic significance

This meeting predates the [[sorted-agency]] engagement (which began Dec 2, 2025) and represents the earliest documented positioning exploration. The "brain booster vs lifelong brain health vs clinical brain drug" question was effectively answered by [[sorted-agency]]'s work in the [[positioning-shift]] — landing on the performance/aspirational frame rather than clinical.
