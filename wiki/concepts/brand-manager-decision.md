# Brand Manager Hiring Decision

Open strategic decision: should [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] hire an in-house Brand Manager, engage a freelancer, or work with an agency? Discussed in [[2026-03-24-meeting-manik-pricing-website|the March 24 meeting]].

## The original proposal

[[thambu]] and [[shivendra-selvam]] proposed: Thambu transitions into a formal Brand Manager role for Lumen / Mindwise, taking ownership of all marketing while Shivendra focuses on sales.

## [[manik|Manik]]'s recommendation: NOT solo initially (from [[2026-04-13-mindwise-raw-dump|Meeting 12, Mar 24]])

[[manik]] **strongly advised against [[thambu]] stepping into the Brand Manager role**. From the meeting transcript:

> "Don't go solo initially. While Thambu has capability, the role requires domain expertise in algorithms, ad mechanics, and marketing execution. Learning on the job will slow the brand during the critical first year."

### Recommended approach
1. **Hire a freelancer or project-based agency for 3-6 months**
2. This person works alongside Thambu, provides direction, builds systems
3. [[manik]] and [[aarti-samant]] give strategic direction to this person
4. After 3-6 months, Thambu transitions into running it independently
5. **Hybrid content model:**
   - Agency / freelancer for performance ad creatives + base of ~10 posts/month
   - AI-generated content + Thambu's own work for the rest
   - Keeps costs manageable
6. **Same agency for ads + creative** — having creative agency and performance agency be separate entities causes friction
