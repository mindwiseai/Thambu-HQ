# Trimacare

Main Indian prenatal supplement competitor to [[aishwarya-chandrasekaran|Aishu's]] [[domains/prenatal/_index|prenatal brand]]. Takes opposite strategic approach — pharma-coded, doctor-driven distribution.

## Key facts

- Founded by Sameer Agarwal & Surbhi Gupta (PlusPlus Lifesciences)
- Launched January 2021
- Patented trimester-specific Multi-Micronutrient (MMN) model (Trimacare 1, 2, 3)
- Uses L-Methylfolate, time-release iron, vegetarian Omega-3
- 5,000+ doctors prescribing, 100,000+ patients
- Pharma-coded branding, doctor-driven distribution — NOT DTC lifestyle brand

## Strategic implications

- Their patent on trimester-specific MMN must be navigated — differentiate with single comprehensive prenatal (like [[ritual-benchmark|Ritual]])
- They own the doctor channel; competing there head-on would be expensive
- No transparency/traceability positioning — gap for Aishu's brand
- No DTC/subscription model — gap for Aishu's brand
- Pharma aesthetic leaves lifestyle positioning wide open
