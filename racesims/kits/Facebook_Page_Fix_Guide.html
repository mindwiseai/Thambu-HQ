Fix 4: Set Up Auto Cross-Posting (15 minutes)

▶

Don't create separate Facebook content. Just auto-cross-post from Instagram using Meta Business Suite.

1

Go to business.facebook.com

Log in with your Facebook account

2

Link your Instagram account

Settings → Accounts → Instagram → Connect

3

Enable cross-posting

When posting from Instagram, toggle "Also share to Facebook" ON

4

For existing posts

Use Meta Business Suite → Content → select IG posts → Share to Facebook

This means every Instagram post automatically appears on Facebook too. Zero extra effort. Facebook page stays active without any additional work.

Fix 5: Get Reviews (Ongoing)

▶

0 reviews = no social proof. Even 3-5 genuine reviews dramatically improve credibility.

How to Get Reviews

  • After delivering a rig, send a WhatsApp message: "Hey [name], hope you're enjoying the setup! If you have a minute, a quick review on our Facebook page would really help us out: [link]"
  • Include a QR code linking to your Facebook review page in your delivery packaging
  • Ask your most active Instagram followers who are also on Facebook
  • Ask Conspit/VNM partners for professional endorsement reviews
Don't incentivize reviews (against Facebook policy). Just ask politely after a positive customer interaction. 5 genuine reviews > 50 fake ones.
