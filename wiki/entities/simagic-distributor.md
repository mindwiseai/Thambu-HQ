# Simagic — South India Distributor Contract

[[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] is the **authorized distributor for Simagic products in South India**. Contract signed 25 February 2025 with Shenzhen Simagic Technology Co., Ltd.

## Contract terms

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Seller | Shenzhen Simagic Technology Co., Ltd |
| Buyer | [[racesims-company|Racesims Solutions Pvt Ltd]] |
| Territory | **South India** |
| Term | 12 months (10 May 2025 -- 9 May 2026), auto-renewal unless 60 days' notice |
| Payment | 100% at order placement |
| MOQ per order | 50,000 RMB (~Rs.5.8L) |
| Annual minimum | 250,000 RMB (~Rs.29L) |
| MAP | No less than 95% of SRP (90% during promo periods) |
| Status | **Active — expires May 2026** |

## Key obligations

- Order credit system: credits from each order offset the next
- Monthly/quarterly sales data sharing required
- **Non-circumvention:** RaceSims must not contact Simagic's OEMs/suppliers directly
- General terms + NDA also signed

## Strategic significance

This contract adds another premium brand tier between [[moza]] (entry) and [[conspit]] (premium). Simagic's Alpha EVO line ($399-$699 MSRP) fills the mid-market gap. South India exclusivity means no [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]] competition on this brand in RaceSims' home territory.
