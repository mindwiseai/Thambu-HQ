# Sim Racing HQ

Bangalore-based sim-racing dealer. RaceSims' distribution partner in the Bangalore market.

**Owner:** [[trayas]]

## Relationship with RaceSims

- **Dealer role:** resells RaceSims hardware in the Bangalore market
- **Customer role:** [[trayas]] personally bought a motion system; install pending as of the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]]
- **Receiving end** of [[jeeva-rathnam|Jeeva Rathnam]]'s simulator transfer — Thambu is disassembling it in Coimbatore and shipping it to Sim Racing HQ
- Install coordination via [[abdul]] (Bangalore-based technical installer)

## Why dual-role dealers matter
The dealer being ALSO a customer is a strong signal — the people selling RaceSims hardware are using it themselves, which is the kind of credibility that VRH's mass-market model can't easily replicate. Can be leveraged in content ("our dealers use what they sell").
