# Fanatec

Globally recognised sim-racing hardware brand (German origin, now owned by Endor AG / licensed under CORSAIR as of 2024). Accessible to [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] in India through the **[[techxhub]]** Authorized Retailer Partner (ARP) agreement — RaceSims does not import Fanatec directly.

## Strategic role in RaceSims portfolio

Fanatec fills the **mid-range direct-drive gap** between [[moza]] (entry tier) and [[conspit]] (premium tier). For customers who want a globally known brand name but don't need the top-end performance or price of Conspit, Fanatec is the recommended slot.

Product lines accessible via Techxhub (per March 2026 pricelist):
- Wheelbases (direct-drive)
- Steering wheels
- Pedal sets
- Accessories (shifters, handbrakes)

See [[techxhub]] for the full Fanatec line INR pricing and [[kit-pricing]] for how Fanatec components slot into RaceSims kit builds.
