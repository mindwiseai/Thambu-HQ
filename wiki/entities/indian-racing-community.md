# Indian Racing Community (IRC)

The **largest Indian sim-racing Discord community**. Identified in the [[2026-03-25-vrh-battle-plan|VRH battle plan]] as a strategic partnership target.

## Why this matters
- Largest concentration of Indian sim racers in one place
- Active community (events, races, hardware discussion)
- **Faster path to audience than building from scratch**

## Strategic options

### Option A: Partnership / sponsorship
- Become **official hardware partner** for IRC events
- Sponsor races, prize giveaways
- Get RaceSims in front of hundreds of active sim racers immediately
- Faster than building own [[discord-community-gap|Discord]] from zero

### Option B: Build own Discord AND partner with IRC
- Both, in parallel
- Own Discord = controlled community + premium features (exclusive content, [[race-engineer-positioning|race-engineer-tips channel]])
- IRC partnership = top-of-funnel reach into the broader community

Per the VRH battle plan, Option B is recommended.
