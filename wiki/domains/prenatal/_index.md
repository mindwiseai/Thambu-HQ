# Prenatal & Postnatal Supplement Brand — India

India's first transparency-led, gynecologist-formulated, DTC prenatal and postnatal supplement brand. Modeled on [[ritual-benchmark|Ritual]] (US, $250M+ revenue). Founded by [[aishwarya-chandrasekhar|Aishu]] (Thambu's wife) after personally experiencing India's supplement trust gap during pregnancy.

**Status:** Pre-launch, research & strategy phase (as of April 2026)

## Core thesis

Indian women rely on imported supplements because local options feel untrustworthy. No Indian brand does Ritual-style transparency + DTC + lifestyle branding for prenatal.

## Key strategic decisions (LOCKED)

- **Lifestyle brand with clinical backing** — not pharma-coded ([[prenatal-dtc-lifestyle-strategy]])
- **FSDU regulatory path** — not Health Supplement category ([[prenatal-fsdu-regulatory]])
- **DTC subscription model** — not doctor/pharmacy distribution initially
- **Gynecologist co-founder** — not advisor, not endorser ([[prenatal-gynecologist-cofounder]])

## Key risks

- **Pricing sensitivity:** Rs.800-1,500/month is premium for India
- **[[trimacare]] patent:** trimester-specific MMN model must be navigated
- **FSSAI/CDSCO line:** all nutrients must stay within ICMR RDA
- **Consumer education:** Indian women defer to doctors
- **Gynecologist search:** must be co-founder, not just advisor
