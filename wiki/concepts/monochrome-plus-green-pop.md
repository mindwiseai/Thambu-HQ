# Monochrome + Green Pop

The locked color scheme for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] visual identity. Decided in [[2026-01-late-meeting-4-brand-identity|Meeting 4]] alongside the [[semi-transparent-bottle|semi-transparent bottle]] hero decision.

## The scheme

- **Foundation:** Monochrome — charcoal-gray or blue (NOT colorful, NOT vibrant)
- **Pop:** Bright neon green from the [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 capsules]] visible through the [[semi-transparent-bottle|semi-transparent bottle]]
- **Logo:** Dark / monochrome with neon-green accent (per [[route-3-architecture-of-intelligence|Route 3 logo direction]])

## Why this combination

### Why monochrome (not colorful)
- Avoids the "doing 10 things at once" feel of [[placebo-wellness-enemy|Placebo Wellness]] competitors
- Reinforces premium positioning (premium brands tend toward restraint)
- Solves the [[age-paradox]] — works for both 18-year-olds (modern) and 80-year-olds (sophisticated)
- Distinct from gym-bro brands (Onit, GNC/Muscletech aesthetic) and from medicinal pharma (white + clinical)

### Why green pop
- The green is **not chosen** — it's the actual color of the capsules. The product itself supplies the color.
- Showcasing it via [[semi-transparent-bottle]] turns the product into the design
- Creates natural intrigue: *"What is this brand?"*
- Energetic contrast against monochrome foundation

### Why specifically charcoal-gray or blue (not green or black) for foundation
- Green foundation + green capsules = monotone, capsules don't pop
- Pure black = too austere, feels morbid for cognitive wellness
- Charcoal-gray = sophisticated + warm + premium
- Blue = trustworthy + calm + tech-coded

## What this rules out

- ❌ Multi-color palettes (Heights territory — too much for Mindwise's single-product story)
- ❌ Bright primary colors (Simply Nootropics)
- ❌ Yellow + black (Onit — gym-bro)
- ❌ Pure white (medicinal pharma)
- ❌ Deep maroon / brown (too earthy / Ayurveda-coded — fights the modern positioning)

## How to use it

✅ Bottle: charcoal-gray/blue sleeve + green capsules visible through transparent body
✅ Logo: dark monochrome with neon-green accent
✅ Website: dark monochrome backgrounds, green CTAs
✅ Performance ads: monochrome backgrounds, green product hero, green CTA
✅ Social templates: monochrome with green accents, never the inverse
❌ Don't mix in additional accent colors (yellow, red, blue tints) — breaks the discipline
