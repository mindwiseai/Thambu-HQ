# Marketing Budget — ₹25 Lakh Annual

Confirmed by [[thambu]] on **February 12, 2026** in the Sorted x Mindwise WhatsApp group, immediately after [[2026-02-12-meeting-5-content-strategy|Meeting 5 — Content Strategy]]:

> "From our end we have decided on an annual budget of INR 25,00,000 to start with. Trust this helps. Our end goal is to have a marketing team in-house where we hire for a content creator, copy writer and designer."

## Budget breakdown (from [[launch-plan-june-1-2026]])

| Category | Amount | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Content production | ~₹10L | Photography, video, brand films, sustenance content |
| Performance marketing | ~₹10L | Meta ads, Google ads, agency retainer |
| Influencer / PR | ~₹5L | Sid Warrier, mom creators, [[freelance-pr-strategy|freelance PR]] |
| **Total** | **₹25L** | Year 1 annual budget |

## What this constrains

- [[pricing-strategy|₹850–900 MRP]] must generate enough margin to support this spend
- Above ₹1,000 MRP would require ₹2-3L/month creator partnerships ([[manik]]'s analysis) — not viable within this budget
- Performance marketing agency retainer: ~₹70K-1L/month + ₹50K-2L/month ad spend (per [[manik|Manik's]] March 10 guidance)
- In-house team is the long-term goal — budget supports transition from agency to in-house over 12-18 months
