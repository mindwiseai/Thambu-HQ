# CTD Module 2 — Mindwise Regulatory Dossier

The **ICH Common Technical Document Module 2** package compiled for [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] / [[cdri-08|CDRI-08]]. This is the **regulatory backbone** of every clinical and safety claim Mindwise can legitimately make. Every marketing claim that touches science must trace through this dossier.

## What CTD Module 2 is

The Common Technical Document is the international format for submitting regulatory data to medicines regulators (FDA, TGA, EMA, CDSCO, etc.). Module 2 contains the **summaries** — the parts a reviewer reads first to understand the safety and efficacy story:

- **Module 2.4** — Nonclinical Overview (animal pharmacology, toxicology, pharmacokinetics)
- **Module 2.5** — Clinical Overview (human trials, safety, benefit-risk)
- **Module 2.7** — Clinical Summary (detailed clinical study summaries)

Mindwise's dossier covers all three.

## Regulatory status
- **TGA-approved (Australia)** — yes (Mindwise / KeenMind branded use)
- **CDSCO (India)** — uncertain status; need to confirm whether the Indian launch route is nutraceutical (FSSAI) or pharmaceutical (CDSCO)
- **Nutraceutical positioning** is the most likely India route, in which case FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) is the relevant regulator, not CDSCO

⏳ **Open question:** Confirm regulatory pathway for the Indian launch. This affects what marketing claims are permissible and what label requirements apply. Should be confirmed before [[launch-plan-june-1-2026|June 1 launch]].
