# Online Instruments India Pvt Ltd

B2B prospect / custom-simulator client of [[racesims-company|RaceSims]]. Based in India. Engaged for a premium, fully enclosed driving simulator as a branded, complimentary guest experience — intended to be installed inside their corporate premises and operated in 24/7 kiosk mode with zero staff intervention.

## Project state (as of 2026-04-23)

- **Original proposal:** 13-slide deck (`racesims/technical/partners/Online Instruments/Online_Instruments_Simulator_Proposal.pptx`) — premium enclosed simulator with custom-clad enclosure, Conspit hardware ([[conspit]] Ares 12Nm + 310 Apex + CPP Apex pedals), RaceSims Pro Cockpit, curved widescreen, enhanced features (haptic seats, in-cabin webcam, immersive audio, Indian road maps, external PIP display, branded idle mode).
- **Pivot (April 2026):** Online Instruments does not want to proceed with the custom enclosure. They want to use an actual **Mahindra Thar** (Roxx variant, based on reference images shared) cut in half, with the front cabin repurposed as the simulator enclosure. Thar becomes the canvas for branding + the guest experience.
- **Delivery model locked:** assembled fully at RaceSims Chennai, shipped as a single piece on a rolling caster base. **No on-site reassembly** per client request.
- **Open logistical question raised by [[thambu]]:** can the assembled unit physically get into their corporate building (doorways, lift, corridors)?

## Site readiness requirements (shared with client)

Produced as `racesims/technical/partners/Online Instruments/Online_Instruments_Transport_Spec.pdf` — 1-page RaceSims-branded transport spec sheet. Covers:
- Minimum clearances along the full transport path (doorways, corridors, 90° turns).
- Service/freight lift minimum (car 3.0 m × 2.0 m × 2.3 m H; door 1.95 m × 2.25 m; capacity 1,500 kg). Passenger lifts will NOT fit this unit.
- Final room requirements (4.5 m × 3.5 m × 2.6 m H, 300 kg/m² floor load, 15A socket, AC recommended).
- Photos + measurements Online Instruments needs to send back to unblock dispatch.

## Strategic relevance to RaceSims

- **First B2B custom-simulator candidate.** Unlocks a new product category — branded-installation, kiosk-mode simulators for corporate/hospitality clients. See [[racesims-accessory-catalogue]] for the adjacent 3D-printing capability that serves this use case.
- **Reinforces [[local-manufacturing-positioning]].** The Thar-based pivot only works because RaceSims can fabricate, integrate, and deliver locally in Chennai.
- **Parallels [[araya-airport]]** — other active B2B installation prospect (airport lounge simulator, ₹6.86–6.96L/unit). Online Instruments is likely priced higher due to the Thar shell + branded livery work.
