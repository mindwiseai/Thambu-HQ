# RaceSims — Project Rules

These rules apply when working on anything RaceSims-related: SEO research, content drafts, kit edits, Discord bot work, competitive intel, vendor docs.

## Brand essentials (always-on context)

- **Company:** RaceSims Solutions Pvt Ltd (always written as one word: "RaceSims", not "Race Sims")
- **Domain:** racesims.in
- **Instagram:** @race.sims
- **USP:** Race-engineer founder. Championship-winning teams background. This is the ONE thing competitors cannot copy (see [[race-engineer-positioning]]).
- **Main competitor:** [[virtual-racing-hub]] (~15× our Instagram following, has Discord, has press, ranks #1 for "best sim racing cockpit India")
- **Brand partnerships:** Conspit (primary), VNM (primary), Moza (entry-level). Authorised distributor + service partner.
- **Owner:** Thambu (also runs Mindwise via separate company [[lumen-marketing-company]])
- **Discord bot:** lives at `racesims/racesims-bot/` (Node.js)

## Strategic state (as of April 2026)

- **The big move:** close the gap with [[virtual-racing-hub]] within 6 months (Instagram from 537 → 10,000+, launch a Discord, win SEO)
- **The unfair advantages:** race-engineer credibility, professional driver focus, sim-to-real training authority, GEO first-mover (AI crawlers already enabled in robots.txt)
- **The execution plan:** [[90-day-execution-plan]]
- **The content gap:** [[seo-content-gap]] — VRH ranks #1 for high-intent buyer keywords with blog content, RaceSims has almost none

## Hard rules

1. **Brand name consistency.** Always "RaceSims" (one word), never "Race Sims" (two words). The SEO audit flagged inconsistent naming as an entity-recognition problem for Google and AI search. If you see the two-word version anywhere in the codebase or content, fix it.

2. **Don't trash VRH publicly.** The battle plan is internal. Public content positions on RaceSims' strengths (race engineer, professional focus) without naming or attacking competitors.

3. **Race engineer voice in every piece of content.** This is the moat. If a blog post or Reel could have been written by anyone, rewrite it to lean into the engineering / telemetry / sim-to-real angle.

4. **GEO discipline.** AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, OAI-SearchBot) are already allowed in `racesims.in/robots.txt`. New content should be FAQ-shaped where possible (FAQ schema has the highest AI citation rate at 10.5%).

5. **Indian market context.** Pricing in INR. Logistics assume Indian domestic shipping. References to UK / US / EU brands must be reframed for Indian buyers.

## Don't

- Don't propose features or content that doesn't lean into the race-engineer USP.
- Don't suggest competing with VRH on their strengths (community size, retail experience). Compete asymmetrically — content depth, technical credibility, GEO.
- Don't generate generic sim-racing content. There's a ton of it on the web. The whole point is that RaceSims publishes things only a race engineer could write.
- Don't confuse RaceSims (sim-racing hardware retail) with Lumen / Mindwise (cognitive supplement). They are separate companies that share the same owner.
