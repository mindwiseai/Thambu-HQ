# Prenatal Contract Manufacturers

Five manufacturers shortlisted for the [[domains/prenatal/_index|prenatal brand]]. **[[tanishq-lifecare|Tanishq Lifecare]] selected as top pick on 2026-04-13** — chosen over [[brukem-life-care|Brukem]] because USDA + EU Organic certifications align with the [[prenatal-dtc-lifestyle-strategy|transparency-led lifestyle positioning]].

| **Lifecare Neuro** | Baddi, HP | WHO-GMP, ISO 9001, cGMP, cGLP | Prenatal third-party | Cost advantages (excise-free zone) |

## Decision rationale (2026-04-13)

The brand positioning is the primary lens. Aishu's brand thesis is transparency + clean label + lifestyle. Tanishq's organic certs let the brand claim "organically certified ingredients" — a wedge no Indian prenatal competitor (including [[trimacare]]) currently holds. Brukem's USFDA + prenatal track record is stronger on regulatory & category experience, but those don't drive the brand story.

## Key evaluation questions (for all)

1. Have they passed actual USFDA inspection (not just registered)?
2. Can they source branded traceable ingredients (Quatrefolic, life'sDHA, Albion chelated minerals)?
3. MOQs for startup initial batch?
4. FSSAI licensing for [[prenatal-fsdu-regulatory|FSDU category]]?
5. Batch-level Certificates of Analysis with heavy metal testing?
