# Prenatal Brand Launch Roadmap

Six-step plan for launching the [[domains/prenatal/_index|prenatal brand]]. Timeline starts from when Step 0 (gynecologist co-founder) is secured.

## Steps

| Step | Timeline | Action | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Step 0** | Before everything | Find [[prenatal-gynecologist-cofounder|gynecologist co-founder]] | PENDING — #1 blocker |
| **Step 1** | Weeks 1-4 | Validate with 30-50 pregnant/postpartum women | NOT STARTED |
| **Step 2** | Weeks 4-8 | Design formulation with gynecologist | NOT STARTED |
| **Step 3** | Weeks 6-10 | Select manufacturer ([[brukem-life-care|Brukem]], Bionova, beRef) | RESEARCH DONE |
| **Step 4** | Weeks 8-12 | Register business + start FSSAI [[prenatal-fsdu-regulatory|FSDU licensing]] | NOT STARTED |
| **Step 5** | Weeks 8-16 | Build brand identity (name, logo, packaging, website) | NOT STARTED |
| **Step 6** | Months 5-6 | Soft launch DTC, seed first 500-1,000 customers | NOT STARTED |

## Critical path

Everything gates on Step 0 — the gynecologist co-founder. Without them:
- Formulation can't be designed (Step 2)
- Brand can't credibly position (Step 5)
- FSSAI application is weaker (Step 4)
