# Araya Airport / Araya Global — B2B Simulator Client

Commercial client for [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]]. Building enclosed airport lounge racing simulators — a significant B2B opportunity that demonstrates RaceSims' capability beyond individual consumer sales.

## Project scope

Fully enclosed, modular racing simulators designed for unmanned operation in airport lounges:
- **Modular cladding** — wrap-ready for branding
- **Kiosk mode** with auto-reset between users
- **Remote management** — zero staff intervention required
- **Software:** Assetto Corsa (commercial licence)
- **Lead time:** 3 months from approval
