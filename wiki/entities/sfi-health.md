# SFI Health

Previous global distributor of [[cdri-08]]-based products, primarily **KeenMind**. Website: sfihealth.com. SFI Health managed the commercial distribution of CDRI-08 under the KeenMind brand across Australia, parts of Europe, and the Middle East.

## The agreement

- **10-year distribution agreement** with [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]]
- **Expired December 2024** (see [[keenmind-agreement-expiry]])
- With the agreement over, [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen Marketing Company]] has stepped in as the new licensed partner for [[cdri-08]] — "Agreement is over. We are coming into the market. Mindwise is our product. Sold in India."

## Strategic implications

The SFI distributor network represents existing commercial channels for [[cdri-08]] products. With the agreement expired, these distributors may:
1. Need a new supplier (opportunity for [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen]])
2. Be approached by a new KeenMind buyer (see [[pier-campanini]])
3. Have existing customer bases that could be converted to [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]

Also see Valentia Biologics S.L.'s role in distributing Glykon in Spain.
