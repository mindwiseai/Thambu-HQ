# Abdul

RaceSims **field technical resource** based in Bangalore. Used for installations across Bangalore, Coimbatore, and other locations where Thambu directs him. Described in the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]] as "a very, very knowledgeable technical person."

## Background
- Previously worked with [[thambu]] at [[arka-motorsports|ARKA Motorsports]] (when it was called **Red Rooster Performance**)
- Transitioned to RaceSims as a field installer
- Carries the ARKA / Red Rooster motorsport technical background

## Role
- **Primary installer** for RaceSims in Bangalore
- **Secondary coverage:** Coimbatore and other locations when directed
- Handles multi-dealer installations (e.g., both [[trayas]]'s and [[lakshay]]'s motion systems during the same week)
- Provides technical backup that doesn't require Thambu to be on-site

## Why he's load-bearing for RaceSims operations

Without Abdul, every motion system install in Bangalore would require Thambu to travel personally. Abdul is the **geographic leverage** that lets RaceSims sell into Bangalore (the largest dealer market) without Thambu being the bottleneck.

The Red Rooster / ARKA background means he has:
- Real motorsport technical experience (not just gaming hardware)
- Familiarity with Thambu's standards and working style
- Trust (long-term working relationship pre-dating RaceSims)
