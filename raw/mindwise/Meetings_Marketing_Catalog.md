These are meetings between the Mindwise/Lumen team (Thambu, Shivendra, Ankita) and Sorted Agency (Aarti Samant, Manik, Tanya, Binita, Avantika). They trace the complete brand strategy evolution from first consultation to visual identity lock.

- **Has transcript:** YES
- **Summary:** Foundational discovery call. Thambu explains the entire Mindwise/CDRI-08 story to Aarti: the CDRI patent, Lumen Marketing Company's 20-year license, the global products (KeenMind in Australia, MemoPlus Gold in Philippines), the SFI contract expiration freeing India re-entry, and the Adam Ritson story.
