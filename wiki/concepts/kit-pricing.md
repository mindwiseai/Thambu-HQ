# RaceSims Kit Pricing Tiers

Full kit pricing for [[domains/racesims/_index|RaceSims]] simulator packages. Four primary tiers, each including sim gear, rig, PC, and monitors. Derived from `racesims/03_Sales/Pricelists/Race sims kits.xlsx` and related kit PDFs.

## Kit configurations

### Tier 1: Rally Setup (~Rs.2.5L)
Entry-level complete sim with single screen. Targets casual-to-serious hobbyists.

### Tier 2: Formula Setup (~Rs.4-5L)
Triple-screen setup with premium peripherals. Targets serious sim racers.

### Tier 3: Formula Cockpit (~Rs.5-6L)
Full cockpit enclosure with formula seating position, triple screens, premium [[conspit]] peripherals.

### Tier 4: Formula Cockpit with Motion (~Rs.11L+)
Top-tier: adds [[vnm]] 3DOF motion platform (4x actuators) to the Formula Cockpit. Targets professional drivers, racing academies, and B2B clients like [[araya-airport]].
