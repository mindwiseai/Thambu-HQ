# CSIR-CDRI Logo Requirement

**Hard requirement** flagged by [[thambu]] in [[2026-03-23-binita-packaging-feedback|the March 23 feedback message]] to [[binita]]:

> "We need to include the CSIR-CDRI logo somewhere on the bottle (it's a credit requirement from CDRI). Can be in any form — small insignia on the side panel, near the bottom, wherever works best with the design. Just needs to be noted in the brief so it doesn't get missed."

## Why
- **Credit requirement** from [[csir-cdri|CSIR-CDRI]] (the patent-holder of the [[cdri-08]] extract)
- Anchors the "government-backed" positioning legitimately
- Without it, the [[your-unfair-advantage|unfair advantage]] claim loses its credibility anchor

## Design constraint
- Can be small (insignia, side panel, bottom)
- Must be **legible enough to be recognizable** as the CSIR-CDRI mark
- Must NOT dominate or push back into the [[scary-drug-problem|"scary drug" / over-information]] aesthetic that Mindwise spent the entire [[positioning-shift|positioning shift]] escaping
- Should integrate cleanly with [[semi-transparent-bottle|the minimal bottle aesthetic]]
