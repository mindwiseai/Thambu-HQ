# IMCD (Mumbai Ingredient Supplier)

Mumbai-based ingredient supplier / distributor. Surfaced in the [[2026-03-26-thambu-voice-brief|March 26 voice brief]] as a strategic meeting target for [[thambu]]'s [[april-2-mumbai-meeting|Mar 31 → Apr 2 Mumbai trip]]. Key to expanding [[lumen-marketing-company|Lumen Marketing Company]] and [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]]'s product roadmap.

> [!note]
> "IMCD" is likely the global specialty chemicals and ingredients distributor IMCD Group (imcdgroup.com). Confirm on next ingest — there may be a specific IMCD India entity in Mumbai.

## Why this is strategic for Mindwise

IMCD is where Thambu plans to explore:

1. **Other extracts** beyond [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 Bacopa]] that Lumen / Mindwise could promote — this is **product line expansion** into new cognitive / wellness categories
2. **[[beta-free-bacopa-extract|Beta-free (bitter-free) Bacopa extract]]** — potentially enables gummy and syrup formulations that the current [[cdri-08|CDRI-08 capsule]] format cannot
3. **Portfolio breadth** — moving Lumen from a single-SKU brand (Mindwise capsules) to a multi-product cognitive wellness platform

## Mindwise roadmap implication

If the IMCD meeting surfaces viable ingredient options:
- **Gummy format** becomes possible for the Philippines market (already asking for it — the [[adam-ritson|legacy MemoPlus Gold market]])
- **Syrup format** opens pediatric + elderly audiences (hard to swallow capsules)
- **New extracts** could launch as sister brands or Mindwise line extensions
- **Supply chain resilience** — a Mumbai ingredient supplier reduces the risk of being CDRI-only

This is the **supply-side mirror** of the Mindwise product roadmap work. [[sorted-agency|Sorted]] is handling the consumer / brand side; IMCD is the ingredient / manufacturing side.
