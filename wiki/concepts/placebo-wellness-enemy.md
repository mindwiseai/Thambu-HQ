# "Placebo Wellness" — Brand Enemy

The named brand enemy that [[domains/mindwise/_index|Mindwise]] positions itself directly against. Articulated across [[2026-01-to-02-meetings-1-to-5|the Sorted strategy meetings]].

## Definition

**"Placebo Wellness"** = pretty labels + weak dosages + borrowed research.

This is the name for the entire category of cognitive supplements that **look** credible but lack the underlying substance. Generic Bacopa brands, cluttered nootropic stacks, and most "brain supplement" SKUs in the Indian market fall into this category.

## What Placebo Wellness products do
- Use **borrowed research** — cite trials done on other brands' extracts
- Sub-clinical **dosing** (e.g., 20-30% bacosides instead of 55%+)
- Pretty packaging that hides the actual formulation gap
- Rapid claims ("instant memory boost") that distrust kicks in when slower results appear
- Stack ingredients to look comprehensive instead of doing one thing well

## What Mindwise does instead
- **Real clinical backing** — not borrowed studies. The [[cdri-08|CDRI-08]] trials were done on the actual extract Mindwise sells.
- **Proper dosing** — 160 mg of [[55-percent-bacosides|55%+ bacosides]] (vs. 20-30% in competitors)
- **Transparent history** — 40 years of CDRI work, not an overnight sensation
- **Single ingredient discipline** — one extract, one job, no laundry list
- **Realistic timeline** — [[8-12-week-onset-claim|8-12 weeks to peak results]], not "instant"

## Competitive landscape mapped

**Tier 1 (direct, Bacopa-based):** Himalaya Brahmi, Organic India, unbranded generic bacopa
**Tier 2 (indirect, cognitive supplements):** Shilajit, Omega-3, Ginkgo, Magnesium, Ashwagandha multivitamins
**Tier 3 (category):** Nootropics, smart drugs, energy drinks, coffee

Mindwise positions OUTSIDE Tier 1 (which is Placebo Wellness territory) by making the [[55-percent-bacosides|bacoside concentration]] differentiation legible to consumers, not buried in fine print.

## How to use the brand enemy in content

✅ Comparison content: "Most Bacopa supplements contain only 20-30% bacosides. Mindwise delivers a clinically optimized 55%+." (See [[2026-03-23-binita-packaging-feedback]] suggestion #5 — this exact line was proposed for Slide 11 of the packaging brief.)
✅ Educational content: explain why dosing and standardization matter
✅ Founder voice: "I built Mindwise because I was tired of placebo wellness."
❌ Never name competitor brands directly — punch the *category*, not the *companies*
