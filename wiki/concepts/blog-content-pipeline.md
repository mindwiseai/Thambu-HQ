# Blog Content Pipeline — 10 Articles

The 10 high-priority SEO articles RaceSims needs to write to close the [[seo-content-gap|content gap]] vs [[virtual-racing-hub|VRH]]. Sourced from the [[2026-03-25-vrh-battle-plan|VRH Battle Plan]].

## The 10 articles

### Buyer's guide tier (highest impact)

1. **"Best Sim Racing Setup in India 2026: Complete Buyer's Guide"** ⭐
   - Target the master keyword
   - Cover every price point: ₹30K → ₹5L+
   - Race-engineer authority edge
   - Direct attack on VRH's #1 ranking

2. **"Best Budget Sim Racing Setup Under 50,000 INR"**
   - Captures entry-level market
   - High conversion intent

3. **"Complete Sim Racing Rig Setup Guide for Indian Homes"**
   - Practical: space, power, shipping
   - Indian-specific considerations
   - Includes [[moza]] entry-level options

### Brand-specific tier

4. **"Moza R5 vs Moza R9 vs Moza R12: Which to Buy in India?"**
   - Brand-specific comparison
   - Captures buyers who already chose Moza
   - Targets the "moza racing India" keyword where VRH currently outranks

5. **"Conspit vs Other Premium Pedals: Why Hydraulic Pedals Are Worth It in India"**
   - Positions [[conspit]] as the premium choice for serious drivers
   - Includes hydraulic vs load cell vs potentiometer

6. **"Conspit vs VNM Cockpits: In-Depth Review from a Race Engineer"** ⭐
   - VRH literally cannot write this — see [[race-engineer-positioning]]
   - Leverages dual partnership with [[conspit]] + [[vnm]]
   - Authority voice flagship piece

### Educational / authority tier

7. **"Best Sim Racing Pedals India: Hydraulic vs Load Cell vs Potentiometer"**
   - Educational
   - Positions RaceSims as the expert
   - Heavy keyword density

8. **"Direct Drive Wheel Bases Explained: What Indian Sim Racers Need to Know"**
   - Technical education
   - High AI citation potential (educational FAQ-shaped)

### Race-engineer USP tier

9. **"How Sim Racing Helps Real Racing Drivers: A Race Engineer's Perspective"** ⭐⭐
   - **The flagship piece.** Only RaceSims can write this credibly.
   - PR-able, share-able, cite-able
   - This is the article the [[yourstory|YourStory]] press pitch is built around

10. **"Sim Racing for Aspiring Indian Racing Drivers: Training Guide"**
    - Targets the parent + aspiring driver market
    - Adjacent to professional driver focus
    - Sets up academy / B2B positioning

## Article quality bar

Each article should:
- Be **1,500–3,000 words** for SEO depth
- Have an **FAQ section + FAQPage schema** (highest AI citation rate per [[geo-content-readiness]])
- Include **comparison tables** where applicable
- Internal-link to relevant product pages (Conspit, VNM, Moza)
- Use **[[race-engineer-positioning|race-engineer voice]]** wherever authentic
- Have a clear headline targeting the keyword in title + H1 + URL slug
- Include at least one image with proper alt text
- Open with a TL;DR + table of contents (LLM-friendly structure)

## Distribution plan

Each article should be:
- ✅ Published to racesims.in/blog
- ✅ Reformatted as a Reddit post (per [[reddit-strategy]])
- ✅ Excerpted into 3-5 Instagram carousels (per [[instagram-growth-tactics]])
- ✅ Excerpted into Discord posts (per [[discord-server-setup-kit]])
- ✅ Pitched as guest post idea to SimRacingBlog.in

This is the **content multiplier** approach — one article → 5 distribution surfaces.
